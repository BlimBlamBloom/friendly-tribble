Example 5:

Passage 1: Email

From: Museum Curator curator@artmuseum.org

To: Art Enthusiasts

Subject: New Exhibit: Modern Art Movements

Dear Art Enthusiasts,

We are delighted to invite you to the opening of our new exhibit, "Modern Art Movements," which showcases the evolution of modern art from the early 20th century to the present day. This exhibit features works from renowned artists such as Pablo Picasso, Jackson Pollock, and Andy Warhol.

The opening event will take place on Saturday, December 3, from 5:00 PM to 9:00 PM. Guests will have the opportunity to attend a guided tour led by our chief curator, Dr. Alice Thompson, and participate in a Q&A session with contemporary artists featured in the exhibit.

Please RSVP by Monday, November 28, to confirm your attendance. We look forward to celebrating the vibrant world of modern art with you.

Sincerely,
Museum Curator

Passage 2: Exhibit Guide

Modern Art Movements Exhibit

Opening Event: Saturday, December 3, 5:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Featured Artists:
- Pablo Picasso
- Jackson Pollock
- Andy Warhol
- Georgia O'Keeffe
- Mark Rothko

Exhibit Highlights:
- Guided tour with Chief Curator Dr. Alice Thompson
- Q&A session with contemporary artists
- Interactive art installations
- Multimedia presentations on the history of modern art

Join us to explore the transformative journey of modern art and its influential movements. Engage with art in new and exciting ways through our interactive installations and multimedia presentations.

RSVP Deadline: Monday, November 28
For more information, visit our website at www.artmuseum.org.

Questions:

  1. Which of the following artists is NOT mentioned as part of the "Modern Art Movements" exhibit?
    • A) Pablo Picasso
    • B) Vincent van Gogh
    • C) Jackson Pollock
    • D) Georgia O'Keeffe
    Show Answer B) Vincent van Gogh
  2. What additional activity is available during the opening event that is not typically part of a regular visit?
    • A) Viewing the art installations
    • B) Attending a Q&A session with contemporary artists
    • C) Exploring multimedia presentations
    • D) Visiting the museum cafe
    Show Answer B) Attending a Q&A session with contemporary artists
  3. What is the purpose of the guided tour mentioned in both passages?
    • A) To showcase the museum's new facilities
    • B) To provide an in-depth look at the exhibit with expert commentary
    • C) To recruit new members for the museum
    • D) To offer free art lessons to attendees
    Show Answer B) To provide an in-depth look at the exhibit with expert commentary
  4. Why is it important for guests to RSVP for the opening event?
    • A) To receive a discount on tickets
    • B) To ensure enough seating and materials for the guided tour and Q&A
    • C) To enter a raffle for a free art piece
    • D) To get access to the members-only lounge
    Show Answer B) To ensure enough seating and materials for the guided tour and Q&A
  5. How does the exhibit guide enhance the visitor experience?
    • A) By providing a list of local restaurants
    • B) By offering background information and interactive elements
    • C) By selling art supplies
    • D) By listing upcoming exhibits
    Show Answer B) By offering background information and interactive elements
