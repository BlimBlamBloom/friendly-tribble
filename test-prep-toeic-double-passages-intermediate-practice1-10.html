Example 10:

Passage 1: Email

From: Mark Taylor mark.taylor@travelco.com

To: All Employees

Subject: Training for New Booking System

Dear Team,

We are excited to announce the launch of a new booking system, TravelBook. This system will help us manage travel reservations more efficiently.

Training sessions will be held next week in the training room. Here is the schedule:

Tuesday, May 4: 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM
Thursday, May 6: 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM
Friday, May 7: 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM

Attendance is mandatory for all customer service and booking staff. Please register at the front desk.

Thank you,

Mark Taylor
Operations Manager

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Employees

From: Sarah White, IT Department

Date: April 27

Subject: Preparation for TravelBook Implementation

Hello Team,

We will soon start using TravelBook, our new booking system. Before attending the training, please:

Back up all current booking data.
Ensure your computer meets the system requirements:
Operating System: Windows 10 or macOS 10.14 or later
Memory: At least 4 GB RAM
Disk Space: Minimum of 2 GB available

Download and install the TravelBook software from the company’s intranet: TravelBook Download

If you need help, contact IT support at extension 1300.

Thank you for your attention.

Best,

Sarah White
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the purpose of Mark Taylor's email?
    • A) To announce a company event
    • B) To introduce a new booking system and provide training details
    • C) To inform about new travel policies
    • D) To provide a schedule for staff meetings
    Show Answer B) To introduce a new booking system and provide training details
  2. Who is required to attend the training sessions according to Mark Taylor's email?
    • A) All employees
    • B) Only IT department staff
    • C) Only administrative staff
    • D) All customer service and booking staff
    Show Answer D) All customer service and booking staff
  3. According to Sarah White's memo, what should employees do before attending the training sessions?
    • A) Update their personal information
    • B) Complete an online survey
    • C) Back up booking data, verify system requirements, and download the software
    • D) Schedule a meeting with their supervisor
    Show Answer C) Back up booking data, verify system requirements, and download the software
  4. Why is it important for employees to ensure their computer meets the system requirements before the training sessions?
    • A) To enhance computer performance
    • B) To ensure compatibility with the TravelBook software
    • C) To comply with company policy
    • D) To prepare for a system upgrade
    Show Answer B) To ensure compatibility with the TravelBook software
  5. If employees have issues with the installation process of TravelBook, what should they do?
    • A) Ask Mark Taylor
    • B) Register for another training session
    • C) Contact IT support at extension 1300
    • D) Visit the training room
    Show Answer C) Contact IT support at extension 1300
