Listening: Daily Routine 1

Passage 1:

Sarah wakes up at 7:00 AM every day. She eats breakfast at 7:30. After breakfast, she goes for a walk in the park. Sarah works from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. After work, she likes to read books. She usually reads for one hour. Sarah goes to bed at 10:00 PM.

Questions:

  1. What time does Sarah wake up?
    • a) 6:00 AM
    • b) 7:00 AM
    • c) 8:00 AM
    • d) Midnight
  2. What does Sarah do after breakfast?
    • a) She goes to work
    • b) She goes for a walk
    • c) She reads a book
    • d) She eats second breakfast
  3. How long does Sarah usually read?
    • a) One hour
    • b) Two hours
    • c) Three hours
    • d) Until the book disappears

Passage 2:

Mark starts his day by feeding his pet fish at 7:00 AM. After that, he practices the piano for 30 minutes. At 8:00 AM, Mark makes a smoothie with fruits and vegetables. He spends the rest of the morning building model airplanes. In the afternoon, Mark takes his bike to the park and rides for an hour. In the evening, he likes to watch documentaries before going to bed at 10:00 PM.

Questions:

  1. What does Mark do after feeding his pet fish?
    • a) He makes a smoothie
    • b) He rides his bike
    • c) He practices the piano
    • d) He teaches his fish to play the piano
  2. What time does Mark make his smoothie?
    • a) 7:00 AM
    • b) 8:00 AM
    • c) 9:00 AM
    • d) At midnight, under the moonlight
  3. How long does Mark ride his bike in the park?
    • a) 30 minutes
    • b) One hour
    • c) Two hours
    • d) Until the bike runs out of gas

Passage 3:

Bun is a small, fluffy dog with pointy ears. Every morning, Bun wakes up at 6:00 AM. He loves to chase birds in the garden. At 10:00 AM, Bun takes a nap in his cozy bed. In the afternoon, Bun plays with his favourite red ball. Sometimes, Bun digs holes in the backyard. In the evening, Bun curls up on the couch and falls asleep by 8:00 PM.

Questions:

  1. What time does Bun wake up?
    • a) 7:00 AM
    • b) 6:00 AM
    • c) 8:00 AM
    • d) Whenever the sun wakes him up
  2. What does Bun do in the afternoon?
    • a) He takes a nap
    • b) He chases birds
    • c) He plays with his red ball
    • d) He joins a doggy dance party
  3. Where does Bun sleep in the evening?
    • a) On the couch
    • b) In his bed
    • c) In the backyard
    • d) Bun never sleeps

After dinner, she watches her favourite TV show at 7:00 PM. On weekends, Lucy visits the local market to buy fresh vegetables. She goes to bed at 10:30 PM.
