Example 11:

Passage 1: Email

From: Dr. Mark Johnson mark.johnson@litconference.org

To: Conference Registrants

Subject: Literary Conference 2022: Workshop Selections

Dear Registrants,

We are excited to announce the workshop selections for the Literary Conference 2022, taking place from July 10-12. Please review the following information to prepare for your participation:

Workshop Registration: Select your preferred workshops by logging into your account on our website. Registration closes on June 30.

Materials: Ensure you bring any required materials listed for each workshop. A detailed list is available on the workshop registration page.

Evening Reception: Join us for an evening reception on July 10 at 7:00 PM in the Rose Garden.

For any questions, contact us at support@litconference.org.

Best regards,
Dr. Mark Johnson
Conference Coordinator

Passage 2: Conference Program

Literary Conference 2022

Dates: July 10-12
Location: City Convention Center

Event Highlights:
- Workshop Sessions: Choose from a variety of workshops on writing, publishing, and literary analysis.
- Keynote Speaker: Celebrated author Jane Austen discussing "The Future of Fiction."
- Evening Reception: July 10, 7:00 PM in the Rose Garden.

Important Dates:
- Workshop Registration Deadline: June 30

We look forward to an engaging and inspiring event. For more details and to register for workshops, visit www.litconference.org.

Questions:

  1. What is the deadline for workshop registration?
    • A) June 28
    • B) June 30
    • C) July 1
    • D) July 3
    Show Answer B) June 30
  2. Which activity is mentioned in both passages as a social event?
    • A) Morning coffee break
    • B) Luncheon
    • C) Evening reception
    • D) Book signing
    Show Answer C) Evening reception
  3. What should participants do to select their workshops?
    • A) Call the conference organizer
    • B) Email their preferences
    • C) Log into their account on the website
    • D) Attend the pre-conference meeting
    Show Answer C) Log into their account on the website
  4. Who is the keynote speaker at the Literary Conference 2022?
    • A) Dr. Mark Johnson
    • B) Dr. Emily Roberts
    • C) Jane Austen
    • D) James Peterson
    Show Answer C) Jane Austen
  5. How does the conference program enhance participants' experience?
    • A) By providing workshop details and important dates
    • B) By offering free writing materials
    • C) By including travel arrangements
    • D) By listing local restaurants
    Show Answer A) By providing workshop details and important dates
