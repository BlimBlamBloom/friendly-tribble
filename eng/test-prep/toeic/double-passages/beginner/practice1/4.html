Example 4:

Passage 1: Email

From: Mrs. Johnson johnson@school.edu

To: All Parents

Subject: School Science Fair

Dear Parents,

We are delighted to announce that our school will be hosting its annual Science Fair next month. This event provides students with the opportunity to showcase their scientific knowledge and creativity through various projects and experiments.

The Science Fair will take place on Friday, May 21, from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM in the school gymnasium. Parents are invited to visit and view the projects from 1:00 PM to 3:00 PM. We encourage all students to participate and explore the wonders of science.

Students who wish to participate should register their projects by Friday, May 7. Each project should include a detailed description, hypothesis, experiment procedure, and conclusion. Prizes will be awarded to the top three projects in each grade level.

We look forward to seeing the amazing projects our students will create!

Best regards,
Mrs. Johnson
Science Department Head

Passage 2: Flyer

School Science Fair

Date: Friday, May 21
Time: 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM
Location: School Gymnasium

Join us for a day of scientific discovery and creativity at our annual Science Fair!

Event Highlights:
Student Projects: View a variety of projects and experiments conducted by our talented students.
Parent Viewing: Parents are welcome to visit and view the projects from 1:00 PM to 3:00 PM.
Awards: Prizes will be awarded to the top three projects in each grade level.

Project Registration: Students must register their projects by Friday, May 7. Each project should include a description, hypothesis, experiment procedure, and conclusion.

We look forward to seeing you there!

Questions:

  1. By when must students register their Science Fair projects?
    • A) Friday, April 30
    • B) Friday, May 7
    • C) Friday, May 14
    • D) Friday, May 21
    Show Answer B) Friday, May 7
  2. Which of the following is NOT a requirement for the Science Fair projects?
    • A) Detailed description
    • B) Hypothesis
    • C) Experiment procedure
    • D) Visual aids
    Show Answer D) Visual aids
  3. When can parents visit and view the projects at the Science Fair?
    • A) 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM
    • B) 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM
    • C) 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM
    • D) 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM
    Show Answer C) 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM
  4. Where will the Science Fair be held?
    • A) School Library
    • B) Community Center
    • C) School Gymnasium
    • D) School Auditorium
    Show Answer C) School Gymnasium
  5. What is the main purpose of Mrs. Johnson's email?
    • A) To introduce a new science curriculum
    • B) To inform parents about the annual Science Fair
    • C) To announce a new school policy
    • D) To request parent volunteers
    Show Answer B) To inform parents about the annual Science Fair
