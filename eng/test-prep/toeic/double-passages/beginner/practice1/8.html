Example 8:

Passage 1: Email

From: Mrs. Williams williams@school.edu

To: All Parents

Subject: School Science Olympiad

Dear Parents,

We are excited to invite your children to participate in our annual School Science Olympiad. This event is designed to encourage students to explore the wonders of science through hands-on experiments and friendly competition.

The Science Olympiad will be held on Saturday, October 9, from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM in the school auditorium. Students can form teams of up to four members and choose from a variety of challenges, including bridge building, chemistry lab, and physics problem-solving.

Parents are welcome to attend and support their children. We will also have guest speakers from the local university and science demonstrations. Lunch will be provided for all participants.

Please register your child by Friday, September 24. We look forward to seeing the innovative projects our students create!

Best regards,
Mrs. Williams
Science Department Head

Passage 2: Flyer

School Science Olympiad

Date: Saturday, October 9
Time: 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM
Location: School Auditorium

Encourage your children to join us for a day of scientific discovery and fun at our annual Science Olympiad!

Event Highlights:
Team Challenges: Participate in events like bridge building, chemistry lab, and physics problem-solving.
Guest Speakers: Hear from experts at the local university.
Science Demonstrations: Enjoy exciting science demonstrations.
Lunch Provided: All participants will receive lunch.

Registration: Register by Friday, September 24.

Don’t miss this chance to inspire a love of science in your children!

Questions:

  1. When is the registration deadline for the Science Olympiad?
    • A) Friday, September 17
    • B) Friday, September 24
    • C) Friday, October 1
    • D) Friday, October 8
    Show Answer B) Friday, September 24
  2. Which of the following activities is NOT mentioned for the Science Olympiad?
    • A) Bridge building
    • B) Chemistry lab
    • C) Physics problem-solving
    • D) Biology field trip
    Show Answer D) Biology field trip
  3. Where will the Science Olympiad be held?
    • A) School Gymnasium
    • B) School Library
    • C) School Auditorium
    • D) Community Center
    Show Answer C) School Auditorium
  4. Who will be the guest speakers at the event?
    • A) Local university experts
    • B) School teachers
    • C) Professional scientists
    • D) Community leaders
    Show Answer A) Local university experts
  5. What is the main purpose of Mrs. Williams's email?
    • A) To announce a new science curriculum
    • B) To invite parents to the School Science Olympiad
    • C) To request parent volunteers
    • D) To inform about a science project competition
    Show Answer B) To invite parents to the School Science Olympiad
