Example 1:

Passage 1: Email

From: Mayor Green mayor.green@townhall.com

To: All Residents

Subject: Town's Annual Funfair

Dear Residents,

We are thrilled to announce that our town's annual Funfair is back! This year, we have an exciting lineup of activities, rides, and games for all ages. The Funfair is a great opportunity for our community to come together and enjoy a day full of fun and entertainment.

The Funfair will take place on Saturday, June 12, from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM at the Town Park. There will be a variety of food stalls offering delicious treats, live music performances throughout the day, and a fireworks display at 7:30 PM.

We encourage everyone to bring their family and friends to enjoy this fantastic event. Volunteers are also welcome to help with setting up and running the stalls. If you are interested in volunteering, please contact our office at volunteer@townhall.com.

Looking forward to seeing you all at the Funfair!

Best regards,
Mayor Green

Passage 2: Newsletter

Town's Annual Funfair

Dear Community Members,

The annual Funfair is just around the corner, and we couldn't be more excited! This year's event promises to be the best one yet, with a wide range of activities that will appeal to everyone in the family. Whether you're a thrill-seeker or just looking for a relaxing day out, there's something for everyone at the Funfair.

Event Details:
Date: Saturday, June 12
Time: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
Location: Town Park

Highlights:
Rides and Games: Enjoy a variety of thrilling rides and fun games with great prizes.
Food Stalls: Savor delicious food from our diverse selection of food stalls.
Live Music: Enjoy live performances by local bands and artists.
Fireworks Display: End the day with a spectacular fireworks show at 7:30 PM.

Volunteers Needed: We are looking for enthusiastic volunteers to help make this event a success. If you're interested in volunteering, please email us at volunteer@townhall.com.

Join us for a day of fun, laughter, and community spirit. We can't wait to see you there!

Questions:

  1. What is one of the main activities mentioned in both the email and the newsletter?
    • A) Cooking competition
    • B) Sports tournament
    • C) Fireworks display
    • D) Art exhibition
    Show Answer C) Fireworks display
  2. Which of the following is NOT included in the Funfair activities?
    • A) Live music performances
    • B) Food stalls
    • C) Rides and games
    • D) Art workshops
    Show Answer D) Art workshops
  3. How can residents volunteer for the Funfair?
    • A) By calling the town hall
    • B) By visiting the community center
    • C) By sending an email to volunteer@townhall.com
    • D) By signing up at the park
    Show Answer C) By sending an email to volunteer@townhall.com
  4. What time does the fireworks display start at the Funfair?
    • A) 7:00 PM
    • B) 7:30 PM
    • C) 8:00 PM
    • D) 8:30 PM
    Show Answer B) 7:30 PM
  5. What is the main purpose of Mayor Green's email?
    • A) To announce the annual Funfair and invite residents to participate
    • B) To introduce new town policies
    • C) To inform residents about a town meeting
    • D) To announce a new community project
    Show Answer A) To announce the annual Funfair and invite residents to participate
