Example 6:

Passage 1: Email

From: Principal Lee lee@school.edu

To: All Parents

Subject: School Gardening Club

Dear Parents,

We are excited to inform you about the launch of our new School Gardening Club. This club aims to teach students about the importance of gardening, sustainability, and caring for the environment.

The Gardening Club will meet every Thursday after school from 3:30 PM to 4:30 PM in the school garden. Students will learn how to plant and care for a variety of vegetables, flowers, and herbs. They will also participate in fun activities such as creating compost and building birdhouses.

We encourage all students to join and get their hands dirty while learning valuable skills. Please fill out the attached permission slip and return it by Tuesday, April 6.

Thank you,
Principal Lee

Passage 2: Flyer

Join the School Gardening Club!

Meeting Time: Every Thursday, 3:30 PM - 4:30 PM
Location: School Garden

Learn how to grow your own vegetables, flowers, and herbs. Participate in exciting activities like composting and birdhouse building. Make new friends and learn about sustainability!

What to Bring: Gardening gloves, a hat, and a water bottle.

Permission Slip Deadline: Tuesday, April 6

Don’t miss out on the fun and educational experience. Join us in the garden!

Questions:

  1. On which day of the week does the Gardening Club meet?
    • A) Monday
    • B) Wednesday
    • C) Thursday
    • D) Friday
    Show Answer C) Thursday
  2. Which of the following activities is NOT mentioned for the Gardening Club?
    • A) Composting
    • B) Birdhouse building
    • C) Cooking classes
    • D) Planting vegetables
    Show Answer C) Cooking classes
  3. What should students bring to the Gardening Club meetings?
    • A) A packed lunch
    • B) Gardening gloves, a hat, and a water bottle
    • C) Sportswear
    • D) Their textbooks
    Show Answer B) Gardening gloves, a hat, and a water bottle
  4. By when must the permission slip be returned?
    • A) Monday, April 5
    • B) Tuesday, April 6
    • C) Wednesday, April 7
    • D) Thursday, April 8
    Show Answer B) Tuesday, April 6
  5. What is the main purpose of Principal Lee's email?
    • A) To announce a new school policy
    • B) To inform parents about the School Gardening Club
    • C) To introduce new teachers
    • D) To request volunteers for a school event
    Show Answer B) To inform parents about the School Gardening Club
