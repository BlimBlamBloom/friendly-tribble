Example 2:

Passage 1: Email

From: Coach Parker coach.parker@school.edu

To: All Parents

Subject: Soccer Team Tryouts

Dear Parents,

I am excited to announce that we will be holding tryouts for our school's soccer team next month. This is a fantastic opportunity for students to showcase their skills, get some exercise, and be part of a team.

The tryouts will be held over two days: Tuesday, September 14, and Thursday, September 16, from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM on the school field. We are looking for students who are enthusiastic about soccer, regardless of their skill level. All interested students should bring their own water bottle and wear appropriate sportswear.

Please encourage your child to participate in the tryouts. It’s a great way to make new friends, stay active, and learn valuable teamwork skills.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact me at coach.parker@school.edu.

Best regards,
Coach Parker

Passage 2: Flyer

Soccer Team Tryouts

Are you interested in joining the school soccer team? Here’s your chance!

Tryout Details:
Dates: Tuesday, September 14, and Thursday, September 16
Time: 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM
Location: School Field

We welcome all students who have a passion for soccer. It doesn’t matter if you’re a beginner or an experienced player – come and give it a try! Remember to bring a water bottle and wear sportswear.

Benefits of joining the team:
Fitness: Stay active and healthy.
Teamwork: Learn to work together and support each other.
Friendship: Make new friends and have fun.

For more information, contact Coach Parker at coach.parker@school.edu.

Questions:

  1. What should students bring to the tryouts?
    • A) A packed lunch
    • B) A water bottle and sportswear
    • C) Their textbooks
    • D) A permission slip
    Show Answer B) A water bottle and sportswear
  2. Which of the following is NOT a benefit of joining the soccer team mentioned in the flyer?
    • A) Fitness
    • B) Teamwork
    • C) Friendship
    • D) Scholarships
    Show Answer D) Scholarships
  3. How can parents or students get more information about the tryouts?
    • A) By calling the school office
    • B) By emailing Coach Parker
    • C) By visiting the school website
    • D) By attending a meeting
    Show Answer B) By emailing Coach Parker
  4. When are the tryouts scheduled?
    • A) Monday and Wednesday
    • B) Tuesday and Thursday
    • C) Wednesday and Friday
    • D) Thursday and Friday
    Show Answer B) Tuesday and Thursday
  5. What is the purpose of Coach Parker's email?
    • A) To introduce a new coach
    • B) To inform parents about soccer team tryouts
    • C) To announce a soccer match
    • D) To provide updates on the soccer team's achievements
    Show Answer B) To inform parents about soccer team tryouts
