Example 5:

Passage 1: Email

From: Zoo Director director@cityzoo.com

To: All Members

Subject: Family Fun Day at the Zoo

Dear Zoo Members,

We are excited to invite you to our annual Family Fun Day at the City Zoo! This special event is a wonderful opportunity for families to enjoy a day full of activities, animal encounters, and entertainment.

Family Fun Day will take place on Saturday, July 10, from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM. There will be a variety of activities including face painting, a scavenger hunt, and live performances by local bands. Our zookeepers will also be giving special animal talks and demonstrations throughout the day.

Members will receive free admission to the event, and non-members can purchase tickets at the gate. Food and beverages will be available for purchase at our various food stalls.

We hope to see you and your family there for a fun-filled day at the zoo!

Sincerely,
Zoo Director

Passage 2: Newsletter

Family Fun Day at the City Zoo

Mark your calendars for our annual Family Fun Day at the City Zoo! This event promises a day of excitement and adventure for the whole family.

Event Details:
Date: Saturday, July 10
Time: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Location: City Zoo

Activities:
Face Painting: Get your face painted by our talented artists.
Scavenger Hunt: Participate in a fun scavenger hunt around the zoo.
Live Performances: Enjoy live music from local bands.
Animal Talks: Learn about different animals from our knowledgeable zookeepers.

Admission:
Members: Free
Non-Members: Tickets available at the gate

Food and Beverages: A variety of food and beverages will be available for purchase at our food stalls.

Don’t miss out on this fantastic event! We look forward to seeing you at the zoo.

Questions:

  1. What is one of the activities mentioned for Family Fun Day?
    • A) Art exhibit
    • B) Face painting
    • C) Sports competition
    • D) Movie screening
    Show Answer B) Face painting
  2. Which of the following is NOT mentioned as an activity at Family Fun Day?
    • A) Scavenger hunt
    • B) Face painting
    • C) Sports demonstrations
    • D) Live performances
    Show Answer C) Sports demonstrations
  3. How can non-members attend Family Fun Day?
    • A) By registering online
    • B) By purchasing tickets at the gate
    • C) By calling the zoo office
    • D) By sending an email to the Zoo Director
    Show Answer B) By purchasing tickets at the gate
  4. Who will give animal talks during Family Fun Day?
    • A) Local volunteers
    • B) Zoo members
    • C) Zookeepers
    • D) Guest speakers
    Show Answer C) Zookeepers
  5. What will be available for purchase at the event?
    • A) Souvenirs
    • B) Food and beverages
    • C) Animal toys
    • D) Zoo tickets
    Show Answer B) Food and beverages
