Example 2:

Passage 1: Email

From: Sarah Johnson sarah.johnson@citymuseum.org

To: Museum Members

Subject: Exclusive Preview of the New Ancient Egypt Exhibit

Dear Members,

We are thrilled to invite you to an exclusive preview of our new exhibit, "Treasures of Ancient Egypt," before it opens to the public. This exhibit features a remarkable collection of artifacts, including statues, jewelry, and mummies, that offer a glimpse into the fascinating world of ancient Egypt.

The preview will take place on Friday, October 7, from 6:00 PM to 9:00 PM. You will have the opportunity to explore the exhibit at your own pace and enjoy guided tours led by our expert curators. Light refreshments will be served.

Please RSVP by Monday, October 3, to confirm your attendance. We look forward to sharing this extraordinary exhibit with you.

Best regards,
Sarah Johnson
Events Coordinator, City Museum

Passage 2: Press Release

City Museum Unveils "Treasures of Ancient Egypt" Exhibit

The City Museum is excited to announce the opening of its highly anticipated exhibit, "Treasures of Ancient Egypt," starting on Saturday, October 8. This exhibit showcases a stunning array of artifacts from one of the world's most fascinating civilizations.

Exhibit Highlights:
- Over 200 artifacts, including statues, jewelry, and mummies
- Interactive displays and educational videos
- Guided tours available daily
- Special events and lectures throughout the exhibit's run

Opening Details:
Exclusive Preview for Members: Friday, October 7, 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM
Public Opening: Saturday, October 8

Join us to explore the wonders of ancient Egypt and learn about its rich history and culture. For more information, visit our website at www.citymuseum.org.

Questions:

  1. What is the primary focus of the new exhibit at the City Museum?
    • A) Modern Egyptian art
    • B) Ancient Egyptian artifacts
    • C) Middle Eastern history
    • D) African wildlife
    Show Answer B) Ancient Egyptian artifacts
  2. What unique opportunity is offered to museum members in the email?
    • A) Free membership renewal
    • B) An exclusive preview of the exhibit
    • C) A private meeting with the curators
    • D) Discounted tickets for future exhibits
    Show Answer B) An exclusive preview of the exhibit
  3. Which of the following is a key feature of the exhibit mentioned in both passages?
    • A) Live reenactments
    • B) Art workshops
    • C) Interactive displays and educational videos
    • D) Archaeological digs
    Show Answer C) Interactive displays and educational videos
  4. What additional activities are highlighted in the press release but not in the email?
    • A) Light refreshments
    • B) Daily guided tours
    • C) Member RSVPs
    • D) Themed merchandise sales
    Show Answer B) Daily guided tours
  5. Why is it important for members to RSVP for the exclusive preview?
    • A) To reserve a spot for the guided tour
    • B) To ensure enough refreshments are prepared
    • C) To confirm their membership status
    • D) To participate in the interactive displays
    Show Answer B) To ensure enough refreshments are prepared
