Example 11:

Passage 1: Email

From: Jennifer Wilson jennifer.wilson@medtech.com

To: All Medical Staff

Subject: Training for New Medical Billing System

Dear Team,

We are pleased to announce the introduction of a new medical billing system, BillPro. This system will improve our billing accuracy and efficiency.

Training sessions will be held next week in the conference room. Here are the dates:

Monday, June 7: 1:00 PM - 2:30 PM
Wednesday, June 9: 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM
Friday, June 11: 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM

Attendance is mandatory for all medical billing staff. Please register at the front desk.

Thank you,

Jennifer Wilson
Billing Manager

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Medical Staff

From: Kevin Brown, IT Department

Date: June 1

Subject: Preparation for BillPro Implementation

Dear Colleagues,

We will soon start using BillPro, our new medical billing system. Before attending the training, please:

Back up all current billing data.
Ensure your computer meets the system requirements:
Operating System: Windows 10 or macOS 10.14 or later
Memory: At least 4 GB RAM
Disk Space: Minimum of 2 GB available

Download and install the BillPro software from the company’s intranet: BillPro Download

If you need help, contact IT support at extension 1400.

Thank you for your attention.

Best,

Kevin Brown
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the main purpose of Jennifer Wilson's email?
    • A) To announce a company event
    • B) To introduce a new medical billing system and provide training details
    • C) To inform about new billing policies
    • D) To provide a schedule for staff meetings
    Show Answer B) To introduce a new medical billing system and provide training details
  2. Who is required to attend the training sessions according to Jennifer Wilson's email?
    • A) All employees
    • B) Only IT department staff
    • C) Only administrative staff
    • D) All medical billing staff
    Show Answer D) All medical billing staff
  3. According to Kevin Brown's memo, what should staff do before attending the training sessions?
    • A) Update their personal information
    • B) Complete an online survey
    • C) Back up billing data, verify system requirements, and download the software
    • D) Schedule a meeting with their supervisor
    Show Answer C) Back up billing data, verify system requirements, and download the software
  4. Why is it important for staff to ensure their computer meets the system requirements before the training sessions?
    • A) To enhance computer performance
    • B) To ensure compatibility with the BillPro software
    • C) To comply with company policy
    • D) To prepare for a system upgrade
    Show Answer B) To ensure compatibility with the BillPro software
  5. If staff have issues with the installation process of BillPro, what should they do?
    • A) Ask Jennifer Wilson
    • B) Register for another training session
    • C) Contact IT support at extension 1400
    • D) Visit the conference room
    Show Answer C) Contact IT support at extension 1400
