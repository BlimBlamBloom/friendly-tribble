Example 7:

Passage 1: Email

From: Event Coordinator events@foodfest.com

To: Food Festival Participants

Subject: International Food Festival Guidelines

Dear Participants,

We are thrilled to have you join us for the International Food Festival on Saturday, March 18. To ensure a successful event, please review the following guidelines:

Setup: All participants must complete their setup by 9:00 AM. The festival opens to the public at 10:00 AM.

Health and Safety: Please adhere to all health and safety regulations, including food handling and sanitation standards.

Booth Decoration: Feel free to decorate your booth to reflect the cultural theme of your cuisine. Prizes will be awarded for the best-decorated booths.

Menu: Submit your final menu to events@foodfest.com by Monday, March 13.

Thank you for your cooperation. We look forward to a fantastic festival!

Best regards,
Event Coordinator

Passage 2: Festival Flyer

International Food Festival

Date: Saturday, March 18
Time: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Location: Central Park

Join us for a culinary journey around the world at the International Food Festival! Enjoy delicious dishes from various cultures, live music, and family-friendly activities.

Highlights:
- Over 50 food booths offering international cuisine
- Live music and cultural performances
- Best-decorated booth competition
- Kid's zone with fun activities

Participant Guidelines:
- Setup by 9:00 AM
- Adhere to health and safety regulations
- Submit final menu by Monday, March 13

For more information, visit our website at www.foodfest.com.

Questions:

  1. What is one of the main highlights of the International Food Festival mentioned in both passages?
    • A) Cooking classes
    • B) Best-decorated booth competition
    • C) Art exhibitions
    • D) Wine tasting
    Show Answer B) Best-decorated booth competition
  2. When must participants complete their setup?
    • A) By 8:00 AM
    • B) By 9:00 AM
    • C) By 10:00 AM
    • D) By 11:00 AM
    Show Answer B) By 9:00 AM
  3. What additional feature is mentioned in the flyer but not in the email?
    • A) Live music and cultural performances
    • B) Health and safety regulations
    • C) Booth decoration guidelines
    • D) Final menu submission deadline
    Show Answer A) Live music and cultural performances
  4. Why is it important for participants to adhere to health and safety regulations?
    • A) To win the best-decorated booth competition
    • B) To ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all attendees
    • C) To receive a participation certificate
    • D) To reduce the cost of participation
    Show Answer B) To ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all attendees
  5. How can participants enhance their booths according to the guidelines?
    • A) By offering free samples
    • B) By decorating to reflect their cuisine's cultural theme
    • C) By providing cooking demonstrations
    • D) By selling merchandise
    Show Answer B) By decorating to reflect their cuisine's cultural theme
