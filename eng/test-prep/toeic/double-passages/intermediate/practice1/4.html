Example 4:

Passage 1: Email

From: Dr. Emily Roberts emily.roberts@medcon.org

To: Conference Attendees

Subject: Medical Innovations Conference: Keynote Speaker and Workshops

Dear Attendees,

We are excited to announce that Dr. Michael Harris, a pioneer in medical robotics, will be the keynote speaker at the upcoming Medical Innovations Conference. Dr. Harris will be presenting on the future of robotic surgery and its impact on healthcare.

The conference will take place from November 15-17 at the Grand Convention Center. In addition to Dr. Harris’s keynote address, there will be various workshops and panels covering topics such as telemedicine, AI in diagnostics, and advances in medical imaging.

Please find attached the full conference schedule and workshop details. Early registration is encouraged as spots are limited. We look forward to seeing you at this groundbreaking event.

Best regards,
Dr. Emily Roberts
Conference Coordinator

Passage 2: Conference Brochure

Medical Innovations Conference 2022

Dates: November 15-17
Location: Grand Convention Center
Keynote Speaker: Dr. Michael Harris
Topic: The Future of Robotic Surgery

Featured Workshops and Panels:
- Telemedicine: Transforming Patient Care
- AI in Diagnostics: Revolutionizing Disease Detection
- Medical Imaging: Innovations and Future Directions
- Genomics: Personalized Medicine

Join leading professionals and experts in the medical field to explore the latest innovations and technologies shaping the future of healthcare. Network with peers, attend interactive workshops, and gain insights from top industry leaders.

Registration:
Early registration is recommended. Visit our website at www.medcon.org to secure your spot.

Questions:

  1. Who is the keynote speaker for the Medical Innovations Conference?
    • A) Dr. Emily Roberts
    • B) Dr. Michael Harris
    • C) Dr. Sarah Collins
    • D) Dr. David Wong
    Show Answer B) Dr. Michael Harris
  2. Which topic is NOT covered in the conference workshops and panels?
    • A) Telemedicine
    • B) AI in Diagnostics
    • C) Genomics
    • D) Nutrition
    Show Answer D) Nutrition
  3. What is the main focus of Dr. Michael Harris’s keynote address?
    • A) Advances in telemedicine
    • B) The future of robotic surgery
    • C) AI in medical diagnostics
    • D) Innovations in medical imaging
    Show Answer B) The future of robotic surgery
  4. Why is early registration recommended for the conference?
    • A) To receive a discount
    • B) To ensure participation due to limited spots
    • C) To get access to additional materials
    • D) To secure accommodation at the venue
    Show Answer B) To ensure participation due to limited spots
  5. What is the purpose of Dr. Emily Roberts's email?
    • A) To introduce new conference topics
    • B) To announce the keynote speaker and encourage early registration
    • C) To provide travel information
    • D) To request feedback on previous conferences
    Show Answer B) To announce the keynote speaker and encourage early registration
