Example 6:

Passage 1: Email

From: Principal Lee lee@highschool.edu

To: All Parents

Subject: High School Science Fair

Dear Parents,

We are excited to announce that our annual High School Science Fair will be held on Friday, January 20, from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM in the school gymnasium. This event is an excellent opportunity for students to showcase their scientific projects and experiments.

Students from all grade levels will present their work, and there will be a panel of judges from local universities and science institutions. Awards will be given for the most innovative and impactful projects. We encourage parents to attend and support their children.

Please note that all science fair projects must be submitted for review by Friday, January 13. If you have any questions, please contact the science department at sciencefair@highschool.edu.

We look forward to seeing you at the Science Fair!

Best regards,
Principal Lee

Passage 2: Science Fair Announcement

High School Science Fair

Date: Friday, January 20
Time: 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM
Location: School Gymnasium

Join us for our annual High School Science Fair, where students will present their scientific projects and experiments. This year’s event promises to be our best yet, with innovative projects from all grade levels.

Highlights:
- Student presentations and demonstrations
- Judging panel from local universities and science institutions
- Awards for the most innovative and impactful projects
- Opportunities to learn about cutting-edge science and technology

Project Submission Deadline: Friday, January 13
We invite all parents and community members to attend and support our young scientists. For more information, please contact the science department at sciencefair@highschool.edu.

Questions:

  1. Who will be judging the science fair projects?
    • A) School teachers
    • B) Local university and science institution representatives
    • C) Parents and community members
    • D) Professional scientists from abroad
    Show Answer B) Local university and science institution representatives
  2. What is the main objective of the High School Science Fair?
    • A) To raise funds for the science department
    • B) To showcase students' scientific projects and experiments
    • C) To conduct science workshops
    • D) To recruit students for science clubs
    Show Answer B) To showcase students' scientific projects and experiments
  3. What additional information is provided in the email that is not in the announcement?
    • A) Details about the judging panel
    • B) The deadline for project submission
    • C) Contact information for inquiries
    • D) Encouragement for parents to attend
    Show Answer D) Encouragement for parents to attend
  4. How can parents and community members support the event?
    • A) By volunteering as judges
    • B) By donating to the science department
    • C) By attending the event and supporting the students
    • D) By participating in the science experiments
    Show Answer C) By attending the event and supporting the students
  5. What is the significance of the awards mentioned in both passages?
    • A) To motivate students to participate
    • B) To fund students' future projects
    • C) To provide scholarships
    • D) To attract media attention
    Show Answer A) To motivate students to participate
