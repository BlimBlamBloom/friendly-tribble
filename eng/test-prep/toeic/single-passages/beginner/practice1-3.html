Passage 3:

Tom has a pet dog named Max. Max is very playful and loves to run in the park. Every evening, Tom takes Max for a walk. They play fetch and meet other dogs. Max is very friendly and enjoys being around people and other animals.

Questions:

  1. What is the name of Tom's pet dog?
    • A. Sam
    • B. Max
    • C. Rex
    • D. Jack
    Show Answer B. Max
  2. What does Max love to do?
    • A. Sleep all day
    • B. Run in the park
    • C. Eat a lot
    • D. Swim in the pool
    Show Answer B. Run in the park
  3. When does Tom take Max for a walk?
    • A. Every morning
    • B. Every afternoon
    • C. Every evening
    • D. Every night
    Show Answer C. Every evening
