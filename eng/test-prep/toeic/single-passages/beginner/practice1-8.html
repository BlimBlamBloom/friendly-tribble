Passage 8:

Emma has a cat named Whiskers. Whiskers is very playful and loves to chase toys around the house. Every morning, Emma feeds Whiskers and brushes his fur. Whiskers likes to sit on Emma's lap while she reads books.

Questions:

  1. What is the name of Emma's cat?
    • A. Fluffy
    • B. Whiskers
    • C. Mittens
    • D. Shadow
    Show Answer B. Whiskers
  2. What does Whiskers love to do?
    • A. Sleep all day
    • B. Chase toys
    • C. Eat food
    • D. Drink milk
    Show Answer B. Chase toys
  3. Where does Whiskers like to sit while Emma reads?
    • A. On the bed
    • B. On the chair
    • C. On Emma's lap
    • D. On the table
    Show Answer C. On Emma's lap
