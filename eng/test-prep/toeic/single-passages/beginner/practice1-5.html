Passage 5:

David likes to play soccer. He plays with his friends every afternoon. David is very good at scoring goals. His team wins many games because of his skills. He dreams of becoming a professional soccer player one day.

Questions:

  1. What sport does David like to play?
    • A. Basketball
    • B. Baseball
    • C. Soccer
    • D. Tennis
    Show Answer C. Soccer
  2. When does David play soccer?
    • A. Every morning
    • B. Every afternoon
    • C. Every evening
    • D. Every night
    Show Answer B. Every afternoon
  3. What is David’s dream?
    • A. To be a doctor
    • B. To be a singer
    • C. To be a professional soccer player
    • D. To be a teacher
    Show Answer C. To be a professional soccer player
