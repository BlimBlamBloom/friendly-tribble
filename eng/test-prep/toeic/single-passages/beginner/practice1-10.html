Passage 10:

Lucy loves gardening. She grows many kinds of flowers and vegetables in her garden. Every morning, she waters the plants and pulls out the weeds. Lucy feels happy when she sees her garden full of beautiful flowers and fresh vegetables.

Questions:

  1. What does Lucy love to do?
    • A. Painting
    • B. Cooking
    • C. Gardening
    • D. Dancing
    Show Answer C. Gardening
  2. What does Lucy grow in her garden?
    • A. Fruits and herbs
    • B. Trees and bushes
    • C. Flowers and vegetables
    • D. Grass and shrubs
    Show Answer C. Flowers and vegetables
  3. How does Lucy feel when she sees her garden?
    • A. Sad
    • B. Angry
    • C. Happy
    • D. Tired
    Show Answer C. Happy
