TOEIC Single Passages - Beginner - Practice 1

Passage 11:

Mark likes to ride his bicycle. Every weekend, he goes for long rides in the countryside. He enjoys the fresh air and the beautiful scenery. Mark believes that riding a bicycle is a great way to stay healthy and fit.

Questions:

  1. What does Mark like to do?
    • A. Drive a car
    • B. Ride a bicycle
    • C. Ride a horse
    • D. Walk
    Show Answer B. Ride a bicycle
  2. Where does Mark go for rides?
    • A. In the city
    • B. In the countryside
    • C. On the beach
    • D. In the mountains
    Show Answer B. In the countryside
  3. Why does Mark think riding a bicycle is great?
    • A. It is fast
    • B. It is expensive
    • C. It helps stay healthy and fit
    • D. It is easy
    Show Answer C. It helps stay healthy and fit
