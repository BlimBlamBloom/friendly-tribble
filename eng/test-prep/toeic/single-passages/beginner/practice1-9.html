Passage 9:

Jack is a firefighter. He works hard to keep people safe. When there is a fire, Jack and his team quickly go to the scene to put out the flames. Jack is very brave and enjoys helping others. His family is very proud of him.

Questions:

  1. What is Jack's job?
    • A. Doctor
    • B. Teacher
    • C. Firefighter
    • D. Police officer
    Show Answer C. Firefighter
  2. What does Jack do when there is a fire?
    • A. Calls the police
    • B. Runs away
    • C. Puts out the flames
    • D. Watches the fire
    Show Answer C. Puts out the flames
  3. How does Jack feel about his job?
    • A. He enjoys it
    • B. He dislikes it
    • C. He is scared of it
    • D. He is bored with it
    Show Answer A. He enjoys it
