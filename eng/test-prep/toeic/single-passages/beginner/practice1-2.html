Passage 2:

Sara is a student. She studies English every day. She reads books, watches movies, and talks to her friends in English. Sara wants to become an English teacher in the future. She works very hard to improve her language skills.

Questions:

  1. What does Sara do every day?
    • A. She cooks food
    • B. She studies English
    • C. She plays sports
    • D. She travels
    Show Answer B. She studies English
  2. What does Sara want to become in the future?
    • A. A doctor
    • B. A lawyer
    • C. An English teacher
    • D. A musician
    Show Answer C. An English teacher
  3. How does Sara practice her English?
    • A. By sleeping
    • B. By studying math
    • C. By watching movies and talking to friends
    • D. By playing video games
    Show Answer C. By watching movies and talking to friends
