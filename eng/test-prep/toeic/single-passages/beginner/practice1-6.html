TOEIC Single Passages - Beginner - Practice 1

Passage 6:

Anna works at a library. She helps people find books and keeps the library clean. Anna loves reading, so she enjoys her job very much. She reads a new book every week and recommends her favorite books to the library visitors.

Questions:

  1. Where does Anna work?
    • A. At a bookstore
    • B. At a library
    • C. At a school
    • D. At a cafe
    Show Answer B. At a library
  2. What does Anna do at her job?
    • A. Sells books
    • B. Writes books
    • C. Helps people find books
    • D. Prints books
    Show Answer C. Helps people find books
  3. How often does Anna read a new book?
    • A. Every day
    • B. Every week
    • C. Every month
    • D. Every year
    Show Answer B. Every week
