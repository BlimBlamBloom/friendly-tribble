Passage 4:

Lisa loves to paint. She has many colours and brushes. Every weekend, she goes to the park to paint the beautiful trees and flowers. Lisa’s paintings are very colorful and she likes to show them to her family and friends.

Questions:

  1. What does Lisa love to do?
    • A. Dance
    • B. Paint
    • C. Cook
    • D. Sing
    Show Answer B. Paint
  2. Where does Lisa go every weekend?
    • A. To the beach
    • B. To the park
    • C. To the mall
    • D. To the library
    Show Answer B. To the park
  3. What does Lisa paint?
    • A. Cars and buildings
    • B. People and animals
    • C. Trees and flowers
    • D. Food and drinks
    Show Answer C. Trees and flowers
