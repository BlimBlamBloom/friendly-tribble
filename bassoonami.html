Hello! Thanks for scanning my QR code at the BMO Vancouver Marathon today. First off, congratulations on running today! Or thank you for cheering! You might have some questions, the first of which being “Why?”. To be honest, after kilometer 30 I also have the same question. So let’s get to it.

To put it simply: “Cho đi niềm hạnh phúc sẽ nhận về gấp đôi gấp ba” - Đen Vâu. I ran once in costume many many years ago and the reaction from fellow runners and spectators was so positive. That energy came back to me twofold. It’s hard these days to find an outlet where you can experience genuine happiness with zero negative impact on others, but I believe that this is one way to do it. So here I am. (Apologies if you have a dinosaur phobia because then you might not feel the positivity).

You are absolutely free to share any photos or videos from today, but I would request that you don’t share my personal information. My name is okay (it’s Liam by the way), but anything else that identifies me (occupation, nationality, etc) I would like to avoid sharing for personal reasons. The focus of this should not be me and I would not like to represent any group of people. I am an individual acting on my own accord.

Anyways this is not meant to be a blog post so let’s get to the point. Great to meet you at the run today. This running is not meant to be for personal gain — spreading cheer and good vibes is the main focus.
