Example 1:

Passage 1: Email

From: Steven Harris steven.harris@techsolutions.com

To: All Staff

Subject: Introduction of New Task Management Tool

Dear Team,

I am pleased to inform you that we will be implementing a new task management tool called TaskMaster. This tool is designed to help us better organize our projects, improve team collaboration, and enhance overall productivity.

To ensure everyone is comfortable with the new system, we will be holding training sessions next week. The sessions will take place in the main conference room. Below is the schedule:

Monday, August 3: 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM
Wednesday, August 5: 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM
Friday, August 7: 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM

Attendance is mandatory for all team leaders and project managers. Other team members are highly encouraged to attend. Please register for a session using the company intranet portal.

Thank you for your cooperation.

Best regards,

Steven Harris
Operations Manager

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Staff

From: Mary Clarke, IT Department

Date: July 30

Subject: Preparations for TaskMaster Tool Implementation

Dear Team,

As we prepare to implement TaskMaster, our new task management tool, there are a few steps you need to complete before attending the training sessions:

Back up all current project-related data and documents.
Verify that your workstation meets the following system requirements:
Operating System: Windows 10 or macOS 10.14 or later
Memory: At least 4 GB RAM
Disk Space: Minimum of 2 GB available

Download the TaskMaster software installer from the company’s intranet: TaskMaster Download

Should you encounter any issues during the installation process, please reach out to the IT support team at extension 1500.

Thank you for your attention to these important preparations.

Best,

Mary Clarke
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the main purpose of Steven Harris' email?
    • A) To announce a company meeting
    • B) To introduce a new task management tool and provide training details
    • C) To inform about a new company policy
    • D) To provide a schedule for upcoming projects
    Show Answer B) To introduce a new task management tool and provide training details
  2. Who is required to attend the training sessions according to Steven Harris' email?
    • A) All staff members
    • B) Only team leaders and project managers
    • C) Only IT department staff
    • D) Team leaders, project managers, and encouraged other team members
    Show Answer D) Team leaders, project managers, and encouraged other team members
  3. According to Mary Clarke's memo, what should staff do before attending the training sessions?
    • A) Update their contact information
    • B) Complete a training module online
    • C) Back up project data, verify system requirements, and download the software
    • D) Schedule a meeting with their manager
    Show Answer C) Back up project data, verify system requirements, and download the software
  4. Why is it important for staff to verify their workstation meets the system requirements before the training sessions?
    • A) To ensure compatibility with the TaskMaster software
    • B) To enhance the speed of their computers
    • C) To comply with company policy
    • D) To prepare for a system upgrade
    Show Answer A) To ensure compatibility with the TaskMaster software
  5. If staff have issues with the installation process of TaskMaster, what should they do?
    • A) Ask Steven Harris
    • B) Register again for the training sessions
    • C) Contact IT support at extension 1500
    • D) Visit the main conference room
    Show Answer C) Contact IT support at extension 1500
