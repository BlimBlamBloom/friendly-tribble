Example 5:

Passage 1: Email

From: John Miller john.miller@factory.com

To: All Employees

Subject: New Safety Protocol Training

Dear Team,

We are implementing new safety protocols to improve workplace safety. Training sessions will be held next week in the safety room.

Here is the schedule:

Tuesday, July 16: 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM
Thursday, July 18: 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM

Please sign up at the front office.

Thank you,

John Miller
Safety Manager

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Employees

From: Peter Clark, IT Department

Date: July 10

Subject: Safety Protocol Preparation

Dear Colleagues,

We will soon implement new safety protocols. Before attending the training, please do the following:

Review the current safety guidelines.
Ensure you have the latest version of the safety manual.
Download the new safety protocol documents from the internal network: Safety Protocol Download

If you have any questions, contact the IT department at extension 101.

Best,

Peter Clark
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the main purpose of John Miller's email?
    • A. To announce a new factory opening
    • B. To introduce new safety protocols and training sessions
    • C. To provide updates on employee benefits
    • D. To invite employees to a meeting
    Show Answer B. To introduce new safety protocols and training sessions
  2. When are the training sessions for the new safety protocols?
    • A. Monday and Wednesday
    • B. Tuesday and Thursday
    • C. Wednesday and Friday
    • D. Thursday and Friday
    Show Answer B. Tuesday and Thursday
  3. What should employees do before the training according to Peter Clark's memo?
    • A. Call the IT department
    • B. Review safety guidelines, get the latest manual, and download documents
    • C. Attend a meeting
    • D. Update their contact information
    Show Answer B. Review safety guidelines, get the latest manual, and download documents
  4. Why should employees review the current safety guidelines?
    • A. To share them with clients
    • B. To ensure they are prepared for the new protocols
    • C. To print them out later
    • D. To send them to the head office
    Show Answer B. To ensure they are prepared for the new protocols
  5. If employees have questions about the new safety protocols, what should they do?
    • A. Ask John Miller
    • B. Visit the front office
    • C. Contact the IT department at extension 101
    • D. Email the safety manager
    Show Answer C. Contact the IT department at extension 101
