Example 9:

Passage 1: Email

From: Emily Davis emily.davis@educo.com

To: All Faculty and Staff

Subject: New Learning Management System Training

Dear Team,

We are pleased to announce the introduction of a new learning management system, LearnPro. This system will enhance our ability to manage courses and track student progress.

Training sessions will be held next week in the computer lab. Here is the schedule:

Monday, April 12: 1:00 PM - 2:30 PM
Wednesday, April 14: 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM
Friday, April 16: 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM

Attendance is mandatory for all faculty members. Please register for one of the sessions via the company intranet.

Thank you for your cooperation.

Best regards,

Emily Davis
Academic Coordinator

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Faculty and Staff

From: Robert King, IT Department

Date: April 5

Subject: Preparation for LearnPro Implementation

Dear Colleagues,

As we prepare to implement LearnPro, our new learning management system, please complete the following steps before attending the training sessions:

Back up all current course materials and student records.
Ensure your workstation meets the system requirements:
Operating System: Windows 10 or macOS 10.14 or later
Memory: At least 8 GB RAM
Disk Space: Minimum of 5 GB available

Download and install the LearnPro software from the company’s intranet: LearnPro Download

If you encounter any issues during the installation, contact IT support at extension 1500.

Thank you for your attention.

Best,

Robert King
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the main purpose of Emily Davis' email?
    • A) To announce a company event
    • B) To introduce a new learning management system and provide training details
    • C) To inform about new academic policies
    • D) To provide a schedule for faculty meetings
    Show Answer B) To introduce a new learning management system and provide training details
  2. Who is required to attend the training sessions according to Emily Davis' email?
    • A) All employees
    • B) Only IT department staff
    • C) Only administrative staff
    • D) All faculty members
    Show Answer D) All faculty members
  3. According to Robert King's memo, what should staff do before attending the training sessions?
    • A) Update their personal information
    • B) Complete an online survey
    • C) Back up course materials and student records, verify system requirements, and download the software
    • D) Schedule a meeting with their supervisor
    Show Answer C) Back up course materials and student records, verify system requirements, and download the software
  4. Why is it important for staff to ensure their workstation meets the system requirements before the training sessions?
    • A) To enhance workstation performance
    • B) To ensure compatibility with the LearnPro software
    • C) To comply with company policy
    • D) To prepare for a system upgrade
    Show Answer B) To ensure compatibility with the LearnPro software
  5. If staff have issues with the installation process of LearnPro, what should they do?
    • A) Ask Emily Davis
    • B) Register for another training session
    • C) Contact IT support at extension 1500
    • D) Visit the computer lab
    Show Answer C) Contact IT support at extension 1500
