Example 9:

Passage 1: Email

From: Marketing Director marketing@techfair.com

To: Tech Fair Exhibitors

Subject: Tech Fair 2022: Exhibitor Information

Dear Exhibitors,

We are excited to welcome you to Tech Fair 2022, taking place from May 5-7 at the Downtown Convention Center. To ensure a successful event, please review the following important information:

Booth Setup: Exhibitors can begin setting up their booths on May 4 from 10:00 AM. All booths must be ready by 8:00 AM on May 5.

Exhibitor Passes: Please collect your exhibitor passes from the registration desk upon arrival.

Presentations: If you are scheduled to give a presentation, ensure your materials are submitted to techfair@techfair.com by April 28.

We look forward to a fantastic event showcasing the latest in technology and innovation.

Best regards,
Marketing Director

Passage 2: Event Program

Tech Fair 2022

Dates: May 5-7
Location: Downtown Convention Center

Event Highlights:
- Over 100 exhibitor booths
- Keynote speeches by industry leaders
- Interactive workshops and product demonstrations
- Networking sessions

Exhibitor Information:
- Booth Setup: May 4 from 10:00 AM
- Exhibitor Pass Collection: Registration desk
- Presentation Material Submission Deadline: April 28

Join us for an exciting event that brings together technology enthusiasts, innovators, and industry professionals. For more information, visit www.techfair.com.

Questions:

  1. When must exhibitors complete their booth setup?
    • A) By 6:00 AM on May 5
    • B) By 8:00 AM on May 5
    • C) By 10:00 AM on May 4
    • D) By 12:00 PM on May 4
    Show Answer B) By 8:00 AM on May 5
  2. Which of the following is a key highlight of Tech Fair 2022 mentioned in both passages?
    • A) Free giveaways
    • B) Industry leader keynote speeches
    • C) Technology history exhibits
    • D) Virtual reality experiences
    Show Answer B) Industry leader keynote speeches
  3. What should exhibitors do upon arrival at the Tech Fair?
    • A) Pay their registration fee
    • B) Set up their booths immediately
    • C) Collect their exhibitor passes from the registration desk
    • D) Attend the opening ceremony
    Show Answer C) Collect their exhibitor passes from the registration desk
  4. What is the deadline for submitting presentation materials?
    • A) April 20
    • B) April 25
    • C) April 28
    • D) May 4
    Show Answer C) April 28
  5. How does the event program enhance the exhibitors' experience?
    • A) By providing detailed setup instructions and event highlights
    • B) By offering discounts on booth rentals
    • C) By including a map of the convention center
    • D) By listing available food vendors
    Show Answer A) By providing detailed setup instructions and event highlights
