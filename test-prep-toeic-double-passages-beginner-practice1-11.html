Example 11:

Passage 1: Email

From: Mark Thompson mark.thompson@store.com

To: All Staff

Subject: New Sales System Training

Dear Team,

We are pleased to announce the launch of a new sales system, SalesMax. This system will help us manage our sales more efficiently.

Training sessions will be held next week in the training room. Here are the times:

Monday, December 2: 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM
Wednesday, December 4: 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Please register at the front desk.

Thank you,

Mark Thompson
Sales Manager

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Staff

From: Linda Brown, IT Department

Date: November 25

Subject: SalesMax System Preparation

Hello Team,

We will soon start using SalesMax, our new sales system. Before attending the training, please:

Save all current sales records on a USB drive.
Check if your computer has at least 1 GB of free space.
Download the SalesMax application from the company intranet: SalesMax Download

If you need help, contact the IT department at extension 202.

Best,

Linda Brown
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the purpose of Mark Thompson's email?
    • A. To announce a new store opening
    • B. To introduce a new sales system and training sessions
    • C. To provide updates on store policies
    • D. To invite staff to a meeting
    Show Answer B. To introduce a new sales system and training sessions
  2. When are the training sessions for SalesMax?
    • A. Monday and Tuesday
    • B. Wednesday and Thursday
    • C. Monday and Wednesday
    • D. Thursday and Friday
    Show Answer C. Monday and Wednesday
  3. What should staff do before the training according to Linda Brown's memo?
    • A. Call the IT department
    • B. Save sales records, check computer space, and download the application
    • C. Attend a meeting
    • D. Update their contact information
    Show Answer B. Save sales records, check computer space, and download the application
  4. Why should staff save current sales records on a USB drive?
    • A. To share them with customers
    • B. To ensure they are not lost during the transition
    • C. To print them out later
    • D. To send them to the head office
    Show Answer B. To ensure they are not lost during the transition
  5. If staff have questions about SalesMax, what should they do?
    • A. Ask Mark Thompson
    • B. Visit the front desk
    • C. Contact the IT department at extension 202
    • D. Email the manager
    Show Answer C. Contact the IT department at extension 202
