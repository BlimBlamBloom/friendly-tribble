Example 2:

Passage 1: Email

From: Emily Johnson emily.johnson@techcorp.com

To: All Staff

Subject: New Email System Training

Dear Team,

We are pleased to announce that we will be switching to a new email system, FastMail, next month. This system will offer better security and more features.

Training sessions will be conducted in the conference room. Here is the schedule:

Tuesday, September 10: 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Thursday, September 12: 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM

Please register for a session at the front desk.

Thank you,

Emily Johnson
Office Manager

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Staff

From: Mark Wilson, IT Department

Date: September 1

Subject: FastMail System Preparation

Dear Colleagues,

We are preparing to implement the FastMail email system. Before attending the training, please:

Save all important emails and contacts to a USB drive.
Check if your computer has at least 500 MB of free space.
Download the FastMail application from the company intranet: FastMail Download

If you need help, contact the IT department at extension 305.

Best,

Mark Wilson
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the main purpose of Emily Johnson's email?
    • A. To announce a new office location
    • B. To introduce a new email system and training sessions
    • C. To provide updates on company policies
    • D. To invite staff to a meeting
    Show Answer B. To introduce a new email system and training sessions
  2. When are the training sessions for FastMail?
    • A. Tuesday and Wednesday
    • B. Monday and Wednesday
    • C. Tuesday and Thursday
    • D. Wednesday and Friday
    Show Answer C. Tuesday and Thursday
  3. What should staff do before the training according to Mark Wilson's memo?
    • A. Call the IT department
    • B. Save emails and contacts, check computer space, and download the application
    • C. Attend a meeting
    • D. Update their contact information
    Show Answer B. Save emails and contacts, check computer space, and download the application
  4. Why should staff save important emails and contacts to a USB drive?
    • A. To share them with clients
    • B. To ensure they are not lost during the transition
    • C. To print them out later
    • D. To send them to the head office
    Show Answer B. To ensure they are not lost during the transition
  5. If staff have questions about FastMail, what should they do?
    • A. Ask Emily Johnson
    • B. Visit the front desk
    • C. Contact the IT department at extension 305
    • D. Email the office manager
    Show Answer C. Contact the IT department at extension 305
