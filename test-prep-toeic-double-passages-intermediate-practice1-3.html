Example 3:

Passage 1: Email

From: Michael Johnson michael.johnson@medicor.com

To: All Medical Staff

Subject: Training for New Patient Record System

Dear Team,

We are pleased to announce the rollout of our new patient record system, HealthTrack. This system will improve our ability to manage patient information and streamline our workflow.

Training sessions will be held next week in the conference room. Here is the schedule:

Monday, October 5: 1:00 PM - 2:30 PM
Wednesday, October 7: 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM
Friday, October 9: 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM

Attendance is mandatory for all medical staff. Please register for one of the sessions via the company intranet.

Thank you for your cooperation.

Best regards,

Michael Johnson
Chief Medical Officer

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Medical Staff

From: Emily Carter, IT Department

Date: September 28

Subject: Preparation for HealthTrack Implementation

Dear Colleagues,

As we prepare to implement HealthTrack, our new patient record system, please complete the following steps before attending the training sessions:

Back up all current patient records.
Ensure your workstation meets the system requirements:
Operating System: Windows 10 or macOS 10.14 or later
Memory: At least 8 GB RAM
Disk Space: Minimum of 5 GB available

Download and install the HealthTrack software from the company’s intranet: HealthTrack Download

If you encounter any issues during the installation, contact IT support at extension 1600.

Thank you for your attention.

Best,

Emily Carter
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the main purpose of Michael Johnson's email?
    • A) To announce a company event
    • B) To introduce a new patient record system and provide training details
    • C) To inform about new healthcare policies
    • D) To provide a schedule for upcoming projects
    Show Answer B) To introduce a new patient record system and provide training details
  2. Who is required to attend the training sessions according to Michael Johnson's email?
    • A) All staff members
    • B) Only IT department staff
    • C) Only administrative staff
    • D) All medical staff
    Show Answer D) All medical staff
  3. According to Emily Carter's memo, what should staff do before attending the training sessions?
    • A) Update their personal information
    • B) Complete an online survey
    • C) Back up patient records, verify system requirements, and download the software
    • D) Schedule a meeting with their supervisor
    Show Answer C) Back up patient records, verify system requirements, and download the software
  4. Why is it important for staff to ensure their workstation meets the system requirements before the training sessions?
    • A) To enhance workstation performance
    • B) To ensure compatibility with the HealthTrack software
    • C) To comply with company policy
    • D) To prepare for a system upgrade
    Show Answer B) To ensure compatibility with the HealthTrack software
  5. If staff have issues with the installation process of HealthTrack, what should they do?
    • A) Ask Michael Johnson
    • B) Register for another training session
    • C) Contact IT support at extension 1600
    • D) Visit the conference room
    Show Answer C) Contact IT support at extension 1600
