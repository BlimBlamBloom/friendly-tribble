Example 6:

Passage 1: Email

From: Tom Richards tom.richards@manufacturing.com

To: All Staff

Subject: Training for New Production Management System

Dear Team,

We are excited to announce the implementation of a new production management system, ProdMaster. This system will help us streamline our production processes and improve efficiency.

Training sessions will be held next week in the training room. Here are the dates:

Tuesday, January 19: 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM
Thursday, January 21: 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM
Friday, January 22: 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM

Attendance is mandatory for all production staff. Please register at the front desk.

Thank you,

Tom Richards
Production Manager

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Staff

From: Laura Evans, IT Department

Date: January 12

Subject: Preparation for ProdMaster Implementation

Hello Team,

We will soon start using ProdMaster, our new production management system. Before attending the training, please:

Back up all current production data.
Ensure your computer meets the system requirements:
Operating System: Windows 10 or macOS 10.14 or later
Memory: At least 4 GB RAM
Disk Space: Minimum of 2 GB available

Download and install the ProdMaster software from the company’s intranet: ProdMaster Download

If you need help, contact IT support at extension 1300.

Thank you for your attention.

Best,

Laura Evans
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the purpose of Tom Richards' email?
    • A) To announce a new production line
    • B) To introduce a new production management system and provide training details
    • C) To inform about new safety protocols
    • D) To provide a schedule for staff meetings
    Show Answer B) To introduce a new production management system and provide training details
  2. Who is required to attend the training sessions according to Tom Richards' email?
    • A) All staff members
    • B) Only IT department staff
    • C) Only administrative staff
    • D) All production staff
    Show Answer D) All production staff
  3. According to Laura Evans' memo, what should staff do before attending the training sessions?
    • A) Update their personal information
    • B) Complete an online survey
    • C) Back up production data, verify system requirements, and download the software
    • D) Schedule a meeting with their supervisor
    Show Answer C) Back up production data, verify system requirements, and download the software
  4. Why is it important for staff to ensure their computer meets the system requirements before the training sessions?
    • A) To enhance computer performance
    • B) To ensure compatibility with the ProdMaster software
    • C) To comply with company policy
    • D) To prepare for a system upgrade
    Show Answer B) To ensure compatibility with the ProdMaster software
  5. If staff have issues with the installation process of ProdMaster, what should they do?
    • A) Ask Tom Richards
    • B) Register for another training session
    • C) Contact IT support at extension 1300
    • D) Visit the training room
    Show Answer C) Contact IT support at extension 1300
