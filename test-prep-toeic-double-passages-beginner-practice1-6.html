Example 6:

Passage 1: Email

From: Maria Sanchez maria.sanchez@bank.com

To: All Staff

Subject: New Customer Service Software Training

Dear Team,

We are excited to introduce a new customer service software, ServicePlus. This software will help us improve our customer service.

Training sessions will be held next week in the training room. Here are the dates:

Wednesday, November 6: 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM
Friday, November 8: 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM

Please register at the front desk.

Thank you,

Maria Sanchez
Customer Service Manager

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Staff

From: Robert King, IT Department

Date: November 1

Subject: ServicePlus Software Preparation

Dear Team,

We will soon start using ServicePlus, our new customer service software. Before attending the training, please:

Save all current customer service records on a USB drive.
Check if your computer has at least 2 GB of free space.
Download the ServicePlus software from the company intranet: ServicePlus Download

If you need help, contact the IT department at extension 102.

Best,

Robert King
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the purpose of Maria Sanchez's email?
    • A. To announce a new bank branch opening
    • B. To introduce new customer service software and training sessions
    • C. To provide updates on bank policies
    • D. To invite staff to a meeting
    Show Answer B. To introduce new customer service software and training sessions
  2. When are the training sessions for ServicePlus?
    • A. Monday and Wednesday
    • B. Tuesday and Thursday
    • C. Wednesday and Friday
    • D. Thursday and Friday
    Show Answer C. Wednesday and Friday
  3. What should staff do before the training according to Robert King's memo?
    • A. Call the IT department
    • B. Save customer service records, check computer space, and download the software
    • C. Attend a meeting
    • D. Update their contact information
    Show Answer B. Save customer service records, check computer space, and download the software
  4. Why should staff save current customer service records on a USB drive?
    • A. To share them with clients
    • B. To ensure they are not lost during the transition
    • C. To print them out later
    • D. To send them to the head office
    Show Answer B. To ensure they are not lost during the transition
  5. If staff have questions about ServicePlus, what should they do?
    • A. Ask Maria Sanchez
    • B. Visit the front desk
    • C. Contact the IT department at extension 102
    • D. Email the customer service manager
    Show Answer C. Contact the IT department at extension 102
