Passage 7:

Michael enjoys cooking. He often tries new recipes from different countries. His favorite dishes to cook are Italian and Chinese. Michael likes to invite his friends over to taste his cooking. They always enjoy the delicious meals he prepares.

Questions:

  1. What does Michael enjoy doing?
    • A. Running
    • B. Cooking
    • C. Painting
    • D. Dancing
    Show Answer B. Cooking
  2. What types of dishes does Michael like to cook?
    • A. Mexican and Indian
    • B. Italian and Chinese
    • C. American and French
    • D. Japanese and Korean
    Show Answer B. Italian and Chinese
  3. Who does Michael invite to taste his cooking?
    • A. His family
    • B. His neighbors
    • C. His friends
    • D. His coworkers
    Show Answer C. His friends
