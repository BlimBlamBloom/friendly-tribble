Passage 1:

John works at a bakery. Every morning, he wakes up at 4 a.m. and goes to the bakery. He bakes bread, cakes, and cookies. Many people come to the bakery to buy the fresh treats. John loves his job because he enjoys making people happy with his baked goods.

Questions:

  1. Where does John work?
    • A. At a school
    • B. At a bakery
    • C. At a hospital
    • D. At a bank
    Show Answer B. At a bakery
  2. What time does John wake up in the morning?
    • A. 6 a.m.
    • B. 7 a.m.
    • C. 4 a.m.
    • D. 5 a.m.
    Show Answer C. 4 a.m.
  3. Why does John love his job?
    • A. Because he makes a lot of money
    • B. Because he enjoys baking
    • C. Because he likes working with people
    • D. Because he enjoys making people happy
    Show Answer D. Because he enjoys making people happy
