Example 7:

Passage 1: Email

From: Brian Carter brian.carter@restaurant.com

To: All Staff

Subject: New Reservation System Training

Dear Team,

We are pleased to announce the launch of a new reservation system, BookIt. This system will help us manage reservations more effectively.

Training sessions will be held next week in the meeting room. Here is the schedule:

Monday, July 22: 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM
Wednesday, July 24: 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Please register for a session at the front desk.

Thank you,

Brian Carter
Restaurant Manager

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Staff

From: Kevin Adams, IT Department

Date: July 15

Subject: BookIt System Preparation

Hello Team,

We will soon start using BookIt, our new reservation system. Before attending the training, please:

Save all current reservation information on a USB drive.
Check if your computer has at least 500 MB of free space.
Download the BookIt application from the company intranet: BookIt Download

If you need help, contact the IT department at extension 304.

Best,

Kevin Adams
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the purpose of Brian Carter's email?
    • A. To announce a new restaurant opening
    • B. To introduce a new reservation system and training sessions
    • C. To provide updates on restaurant policies
    • D. To invite staff to a meeting
    Show Answer B. To introduce a new reservation system and training sessions
  2. When are the training sessions for BookIt?
    • A. Monday and Tuesday
    • B. Wednesday and Thursday
    • C. Monday and Wednesday
    • D. Tuesday and Thursday
    Show Answer C. Monday and Wednesday
  3. What should staff do before the training according to Kevin Adams' memo?
    • A. Call the IT department
    • B. Save reservation information, check computer space, and download the application
    • C. Attend a meeting
    • D. Update their contact information
    Show Answer B. Save reservation information, check computer space, and download the application
  4. Why should staff save current reservation information on a USB drive?
    • A. To share it with customers
    • B. To ensure it is not lost during the transition
    • C. To print it out later
    • D. To send it to the head office
    Show Answer B. To ensure it is not lost during the transition
  5. If staff have questions about BookIt, what should they do?
    • A. Ask Brian Carter
    • B. Visit the front desk
    • C. Contact the IT department at extension 304
    • D. Email the manager
    Show Answer C. Contact the IT department at extension 304
