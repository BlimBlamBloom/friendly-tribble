Example 7:

Passage 1: Email

From: Dr. Wright wright@hospital.com

To: All Staff

Subject: Hospital Charity Run

Dear Staff,

We are pleased to announce the upcoming Hospital Charity Run, an event to raise funds for the new children's wing. This is a great opportunity for us to come together, promote health and wellness, and support a meaningful cause.

The Charity Run will take place on Sunday, August 22, starting at 8:00 AM. Participants can choose between a 5K run and a 10K run. The event will start and finish at the hospital's main entrance. There will be water stations along the route and a celebratory breakfast at the end of the run.

We encourage all staff to participate or volunteer. Registration is open now at the front desk. Please sign up by Friday, August 13.

Thank you for your support.

Best regards,
Dr. Wright

Passage 2: Flyer

Hospital Charity Run

Date: Sunday, August 22
Time: 8:00 AM
Location: Hospital Main Entrance

Join us for the Hospital Charity Run to raise funds for the new children's wing. Choose between a 5K or 10K run and enjoy a morning of fitness and community spirit.

Event Highlights:
5K and 10K Runs: Select the distance that suits you best.
Water Stations: Stay hydrated with water stations along the route.
Celebratory Breakfast: Enjoy a delicious breakfast after the run.

Registration: Sign up at the front desk by Friday, August 13.

Let’s make this a successful event for a great cause!

Questions:

  1. When will the Hospital Charity Run take place?
    • A) Saturday, August 21
    • B) Sunday, August 22
    • C) Monday, August 23
    • D) Tuesday, August 24
    Show Answer B) Sunday, August 22
  2. Which of the following is NOT mentioned as part of the event highlights?
    • A) Celebratory breakfast
    • B) Water stations
    • C) Awards ceremony
    • D) 5K and 10K runs
    Show Answer C) Awards ceremony
  3. How can staff sign up for the Charity Run?
    • A) By calling the hospital office
    • B) By visiting the hospital website
    • C) By signing up at the front desk
    • D) By sending an email to Dr. Wright
    Show Answer C) By signing up at the front desk
  4. What time does the Charity Run start?
    • A) 7:00 AM
    • B) 7:30 AM
    • C) 8:00 AM
    • D) 8:30 AM
    Show Answer C) 8:00 AM
  5. What is the main purpose of Dr. Wright's email?
    • A) To announce a new hospital policy
    • B) To invite staff to the Hospital Charity Run
    • C) To inform staff about a new fitness program
    • D) To introduce new hospital staff
    Show Answer B) To invite staff to the Hospital Charity Run
