Example 3:

Passage 1: Email

From: Library Director director@townlibrary.com

To: All Members

Subject: Summer Reading Program

Dear Library Members,

We are excited to announce the launch of our annual Summer Reading Program! This year’s program is packed with fun activities, reading challenges, and great prizes for participants of all ages.

The Summer Reading Program will run from June 1 to August 31. Participants can sign up at the library or online through our website. Throughout the summer, we will host various events such as book clubs, storytelling sessions, and author visits.

We encourage everyone to join the program and make reading a part of their summer routine. To kick off the program, we will have a special event on Saturday, June 5, at 10:00 AM in the library’s main hall. There will be refreshments, games, and a special guest author.

We look forward to seeing you at the library this summer!

Warm regards,
Library Director

Passage 2: Newsletter

Summer Reading Program

Join our exciting Summer Reading Program at the Town Library! This year’s program offers something for everyone, whether you’re a young reader or a seasoned book lover.

Program Dates: June 1 - August 31

Kick-Off Event:
Date: Saturday, June 5
Time: 10:00 AM
Location: Library Main Hall

Activities:
Book Clubs: Join a book club and discuss your favorite books with others.
Storytelling Sessions: Enjoy storytelling sessions for children and families.
Author Visits: Meet and greet with guest authors.

How to Participate: Sign up at the library or online at www.townlibrary.com. Complete reading challenges to earn points and win prizes!

Don’t miss out on the fun and make reading a highlight of your summer.

Questions:

  1. How long will the Summer Reading Program last?
    • A) One month
    • B) Two months
    • C) Three months
    • D) Four months
    Show Answer C) Three months
  2. Which of the following activities is NOT mentioned as part of the Summer Reading Program?
    • A) Book clubs
    • B) Storytelling sessions
    • C) Cooking classes
    • D) Author visits
    Show Answer C) Cooking classes
  3. Where will the kick-off event for the Summer Reading Program be held?
    • A) Community Center
    • B) Library Main Hall
    • C) Town Park
    • D) School Auditorium
    Show Answer B) Library Main Hall
  4. What is the main purpose of the Library Director's email?
    • A) To introduce new library policies
    • B) To announce the Summer Reading Program
    • C) To inform about library renovations
    • D) To invite members to a book sale
    Show Answer B) To announce the Summer Reading Program
  5. How can participants sign up for the Summer Reading Program?
    • A) By calling the library
    • B) By visiting the library or online at www.townlibrary.com
    • C) By emailing the Library Director
    • D) By sending a letter to the library
    Show Answer B) By visiting the library or online at www.townlibrary.com
