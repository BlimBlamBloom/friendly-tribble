Example 3:

Passage 1: Email

From: David Green david.green@company.com

To: All Employees

Subject: New Time-Tracking Software Training

Hello Everyone,

We are implementing a new time-tracking software called TimeKeeper. This will help us keep better records of work hours and improve accuracy.

Training sessions will be held next week in the training room. Here are the times:

Wednesday, October 9: 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM
Friday, October 11: 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM

Please sign up at the main office.

Thank you,

David Green
HR Manager

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Employees

From: Lisa Brown, IT Department

Date: October 1

Subject: TimeKeeper Software Preparation

Dear Team,

We will soon start using TimeKeeper for time-tracking. Before the training, please do the following:

Save all current time-tracking records on a USB drive.
Ensure your computer has at least 1 GB of free space.
Download the TimeKeeper software from the internal network: TimeKeeper Download

If you have questions, please call the IT department at extension 404.

Best,

Lisa Brown
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the purpose of David Green's email?
    • A. To announce a new office policy
    • B. To introduce new time-tracking software and training sessions
    • C. To provide updates on work schedules
    • D. To invite employees to a team-building event
    Show Answer B. To introduce new time-tracking software and training sessions
  2. When are the training sessions for TimeKeeper?
    • A. Tuesday and Thursday
    • B. Monday and Wednesday
    • C. Wednesday and Friday
    • D. Thursday and Friday
    Show Answer C. Wednesday and Friday
  3. What should employees do before the training according to Lisa Brown's memo?
    • A. Call the IT department
    • B. Save time-tracking records, check computer space, and download the software
    • C. Attend a meeting
    • D. Update their contact information
    Show Answer B. Save time-tracking records, check computer space, and download the software
  4. Why should employees save current time-tracking records on a USB drive?
    • A. To share them with clients
    • B. To prevent data loss during the transition
    • C. To print them out later
    • D. To send them to the HR department
    Show Answer B. To prevent data loss during the transition
  5. If employees have questions about TimeKeeper, what should they do?
    • A. Ask David Green
    • B. Visit the main office
    • C. Call the IT department at extension 404
    • D. Email the HR manager
    Show Answer C. Call the IT department at extension 404
