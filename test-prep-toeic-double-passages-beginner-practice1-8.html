Example 8:

Passage 1: Email

From: Lisa Green lisa.green@library.com

To: All Staff

Subject: New Cataloging System Training

Dear Team,

We are excited to announce that we will be implementing a new cataloging system, LibCatalog. This system will help us manage our library resources better.

Training sessions will be held next week in the conference room. Here are the times:

Tuesday, October 15: 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM
Thursday, October 17: 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM

Please sign up at the front desk.

Thank you,

Lisa Green
Library Director

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Staff

From: John White, IT Department

Date: October 10

Subject: LibCatalog System Preparation

Dear Colleagues,

We will soon start using LibCatalog, our new cataloging system. Before the training, please:

Save all current catalog records on a USB drive.
Ensure your computer has at least 1 GB of free space.
Download the LibCatalog software from the internal network: LibCatalog Download

If you have questions, please call the IT department at extension 201.

Best,

John White
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the main purpose of Lisa Green's email?
    • A. To announce a new library branch opening
    • B. To introduce a new cataloging system and training sessions
    • C. To provide updates on library policies
    • D. To invite staff to a meeting
    Show Answer B. To introduce a new cataloging system and training sessions
  2. When are the training sessions for LibCatalog?
    • A. Monday and Wednesday
    • B. Tuesday and Thursday
    • C. Wednesday and Friday
    • D. Thursday and Friday
    Show Answer B. Tuesday and Thursday
  3. What should staff do before the training according to John White's memo?
    • A. Call the IT department
    • B. Save catalog records, check computer space, and download the software
    • C. Attend a meeting
    • D. Update their contact information
    Show Answer B. Save catalog records, check computer space, and download the software
  4. Why should staff save current catalog records on a USB drive?
    • A. To share them with patrons
    • B. To prevent data loss during the transition
    • C. To print them out later
    • D. To send them to the head office
    Show Answer B. To prevent data loss during the transition
  5. If staff have questions about LibCatalog, what should they do?
    • A. Ask Lisa Green
    • B. Visit the front desk
    • C. Call the IT department at extension 201
    • D. Email the library director
    Show Answer C. Call the IT department at extension 201
