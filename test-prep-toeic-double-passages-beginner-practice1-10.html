Example 10:

Passage 1: Email

From: Anna White anna.white@school.com

To: All Teachers

Subject: New Grading System Training

Dear Team,

We are excited to announce the implementation of a new grading system, GradePro. This system will help us manage grades more effectively.

Training sessions will be held next week in the computer lab. Here is the schedule:

Tuesday, November 12: 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM
Thursday, November 14: 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM

Please sign up at the front office.

Thank you,

Anna White
Principal

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Teachers

From: Michael Green, IT Department

Date: November 5

Subject: GradePro System Preparation

Dear Colleagues,

We will soon start using GradePro, our new grading system. Before attending the training, please:

Save all current grade records on a USB drive.
Ensure your computer has at least 1 GB of free space.
Download the GradePro software from the internal network: GradePro Download

If you have questions, please call the IT department at extension 205.

Best,

Michael Green
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the main purpose of Anna White's email?
    • A. To announce a new school opening
    • B. To introduce a new grading system and training sessions
    • C. To provide updates on school policies
    • D. To invite teachers to a meeting
    Show Answer B. To introduce a new grading system and training sessions
  2. When are the training sessions for GradePro?
    • A. Monday and Wednesday
    • B. Tuesday and Thursday
    • C. Wednesday and Friday
    • D. Thursday and Friday
    Show Answer B. Tuesday and Thursday
  3. What should teachers do before the training according to Michael Green's memo?
    • A. Call the IT department
    • B. Save grade records, check computer space, and download the software
    • C. Attend a meeting
    • D. Update their contact information
    Show Answer B. Save grade records, check computer space, and download the software
  4. Why should teachers save current grade records on a USB drive?
    • A. To share them with students
    • B. To prevent data loss during the transition
    • C. To print them out later
    • D. To send them to the head office
    Show Answer B. To prevent data loss during the transition
  5. If teachers have questions about GradePro, what should they do?
    • A. Ask Anna White
    • B. Visit the front office
    • C. Call the IT department at extension 205
    • D. Email the principal
    Show Answer C. Call the IT department at extension 205
