Example 10:

Passage 1: Email

From: Dr. Samantha Lee samantha.lee@healthsymposium.com

To: Symposium Attendees

Subject: Health and Wellness Symposium: Final Details

Dear Attendees,

We are looking forward to welcoming you to the Health and Wellness Symposium on June 12-14. Here are some final details to ensure you have a productive and enjoyable experience:

Registration: Opens at 8:00 AM on June 12 at the main entrance of the Grand Hotel.

Sessions: The symposium features keynote addresses, panel discussions, and hands-on workshops. Please refer to the attached schedule for session times and locations.

Networking Reception: Join us for a networking reception on June 12 at 6:00 PM in the Grand Ballroom.

For any last-minute questions, please contact us at info@healthsymposium.com.

Best regards,
Dr. Samantha Lee
Symposium Coordinator

Passage 2: Event Schedule

Health and Wellness Symposium

Dates: June 12-14
Location: Grand Hotel

Schedule Highlights:
- June 12: Registration opens at 8:00 AM; Networking Reception at 6:00 PM
- June 13: Keynote addresses and panel discussions
- June 14: Hands-on workshops and closing ceremony

Keynote Speakers:
- Dr. James Peterson: "The Future of Integrative Medicine"
- Dr. Laura Martinez: "Advancements in Mental Health Treatments"

We look forward to three days of insightful discussions, learning opportunities, and networking. For the full schedule, visit www.healthsymposium.com.

Questions:

  1. What time does registration open on the first day of the symposium?
    • A) 7:00 AM
    • B) 8:00 AM
    • C) 9:00 AM
    • D) 10:00 AM
    Show Answer B) 8:00 AM
  2. Which of the following activities is NOT included in the symposium schedule?
    • A) Keynote addresses
    • B) Panel discussions
    • C) Cooking demonstrations
    • D) Hands-on workshops
    Show Answer C) Cooking demonstrations
  3. What is the purpose of the networking reception mentioned in both passages?
    • A) To celebrate the opening of the symposium
    • B) To provide a platform for attendees to connect
    • C) To present awards to keynote speakers
    • D) To offer additional training sessions
    Show Answer B) To provide a platform for attendees to connect
  4. Who is one of the keynote speakers at the symposium?
    • A) Dr. Samantha Lee
    • B) Dr. James Peterson
    • C) Dr. Emily Roberts
    • D) Dr. Michael Harris
    Show Answer B) Dr. James Peterson
  5. How does the event schedule enhance the attendees' experience?
    • A) By listing meal options
    • B) By providing a detailed overview of sessions and events
    • C) By offering discounts on future symposiums
    • D) By including transportation information
    Show Answer B) By providing a detailed overview of sessions and events
