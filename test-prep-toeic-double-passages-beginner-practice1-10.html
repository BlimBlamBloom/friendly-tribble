Example 10:

Passage 1: Email

From: Chef Ramirez ramirez@culinaryschool.edu

To: All Students

Subject: Cooking Class with Celebrity Chef

Dear Students,

We are excited to announce a special cooking class with renowned celebrity chef, Chef Kyle! This is a unique opportunity to learn from one of the best in the culinary world.

The class will be held on Wednesday, March 10, from 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM in the culinary school's main kitchen. Chef Kyle will demonstrate some of his signature dishes and share his tips and techniques. There will also be a hands-on session where you can practice what you learn.

Space is limited, so please register by Friday, March 5, to secure your spot. This is an event you don’t want to miss!

Best regards,
Chef Ramirez
Culinary School Director

Passage 2: Flyer

Cooking Class with Celebrity Chef Kyle

Date: Wednesday, March 10
Time: 4:00 PM - 7:00 PM
Location: Culinary School Main Kitchen

Join us for a special cooking class with Chef Kyle, one of the most celebrated chefs in the culinary world. Learn how to create his signature dishes and gain valuable cooking tips.

Event Highlights:
Live Demonstration: Watch Chef Kyle prepare some of his famous recipes.
Hands-On Session: Get hands-on experience and cook alongside Chef Kyle.
Exclusive Tips: Learn professional cooking techniques and tips.

Registration: Register by Friday, March 5, to secure your spot.

Don’t miss this incredible opportunity to learn from the best!

Questions:

  1. When is the registration deadline for the cooking class?
    • A) Friday, February 26
    • B) Friday, March 5
    • C) Friday, March 12
    • D) Friday, March 19
    Show Answer B) Friday, March 5
  2. Which of the following activities is NOT mentioned as part of the cooking class?
    • A) Live demonstration
    • B) Hands-on session
    • C) Exclusive tips
    • D) Baking competition
    Show Answer D) Baking competition
  3. Where will the cooking class be held?
    • A) School Cafeteria
    • B) Community Center
    • C) Culinary School Main Kitchen
    • D) School Auditorium
    Show Answer C) Culinary School Main Kitchen
  4. What time will the cooking class take place?
    • A) 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM
    • B) 4:00 PM - 7:00 PM
    • C) 5:00 PM - 8:00 PM
    • D) 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM
    Show Answer B) 4:00 PM - 7:00 PM
  5. What is the main purpose of Chef Ramirez's email?
    • A) To introduce a new culinary course
    • B) To invite students to a cooking class with a celebrity chef
    • C) To announce a cooking competition
    • D) To inform about kitchen renovations
    Show Answer B) To invite students to a cooking class with a celebrity chef
