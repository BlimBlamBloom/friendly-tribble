Example 2:

Passage 1: Email

From: Rachel Green rachel.green@foodco.com

To: All Employees

Subject: Implementation of New Inventory Tracking System

Dear Team,

I am excited to announce that we are introducing a new inventory tracking system called StockMaster. This system will help us monitor our stock levels more accurately and streamline our inventory management process.

To ensure everyone is familiar with the new system, training sessions will be held next week in the training center. The schedule is as follows:

Tuesday, September 8: 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM
Thursday, September 10: 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM
Friday, September 11: 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM

Attendance is mandatory for all warehouse and logistics staff. Other employees are encouraged to attend if their schedules allow. Please sign up for a session using the company intranet.

Thank you for your cooperation.

Best regards,

Rachel Green
Logistics Manager

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Employees

From: John Miller, IT Department

Date: September 2

Subject: Preparations for StockMaster System Implementation

Dear Team,

As we prepare to implement StockMaster, our new inventory tracking system, please complete the following steps before attending the training sessions:

Back up all current inventory data.
Ensure your computer meets the system requirements:
Operating System: Windows 10 or macOS 10.14 or later
Memory: At least 4 GB RAM
Disk Space: Minimum of 3 GB available

Download and install the StockMaster software from the company’s intranet: StockMaster Download

If you encounter any issues during the installation, contact IT support at extension 1400.

Thank you for your attention.

Best,

John Miller
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the main purpose of Rachel Green's email?
    • A) To announce a company event
    • B) To introduce a new inventory tracking system and provide training details
    • C) To inform about a new policy
    • D) To provide a schedule for staff meetings
    Show Answer B) To introduce a new inventory tracking system and provide training details
  2. Who must attend the training sessions according to Rachel Green's email?
    • A) All employees
    • B) Only IT staff
    • C) Only warehouse and logistics staff
    • D) Only managers
    Show Answer C) Only warehouse and logistics staff
  3. According to John Miller's memo, what should employees do before attending the training sessions?
    • A) Update their personal information
    • B) Complete an online survey
    • C) Back up inventory data, verify system requirements, and download the software
    • D) Schedule a meeting with their supervisor
    Show Answer C) Back up inventory data, verify system requirements, and download the software
  4. Why is it important for employees to ensure their computer meets the system requirements before the training sessions?
    • A) To enhance computer performance
    • B) To ensure compatibility with the StockMaster software
    • C) To comply with company policy
    • D) To prepare for a system upgrade
    Show Answer B) To ensure compatibility with the StockMaster software
  5. If employees have issues with the installation process of StockMaster, what should they do?
    • A) Ask Rachel Green
    • B) Register for another training session
    • C) Contact IT support at extension 1400
    • D) Visit the training center
    Show Answer C) Contact IT support at extension 1400
