Example 9:

Passage 1: Email

From: Mr. Garcia garcia@school.edu

To: All Parents

Subject: School Talent Show

Dear Parents,

We are thrilled to announce our upcoming School Talent Show, an event where students can showcase their unique talents and perform in front of their peers, teachers, and parents.

The Talent Show will take place on Friday, November 12, from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM in the school auditorium. Students can sign up to perform individually or in groups. We welcome a wide range of talents, including singing, dancing, magic tricks, comedy, and more.

Please encourage your child to participate. It’s a wonderful opportunity for them to build confidence and have fun. Parents are also invited to attend and cheer for their children. Light refreshments will be available.

To sign up, students should submit their act details by Monday, November 1. We look forward to an evening of amazing performances!

Best regards,
Mr. Garcia
Event Coordinator

Passage 2: Flyer

School Talent Show

Date: Friday, November 12
Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
Location: School Auditorium

Show off your talent at the School Talent Show! Whether you sing, dance, perform magic, or have another unique talent, we want to see it!

Event Highlights:
Student Performances: Enjoy a variety of performances by our talented students.
Parent Attendance: Parents are welcome to come and support their children.
Refreshments: Light refreshments will be available.

Sign-Up Deadline: Submit your act details by Monday, November 1.

Don’t miss this chance to shine on stage!

Questions:

  1. When is the sign-up deadline for the Talent Show?
    • A) Monday, October 25
    • B) Monday, November 1
    • C) Monday, November 8
    • D) Monday, November 15
    Show Answer B) Monday, November 1
  2. Which of the following is NOT mentioned as a type of performance for the Talent Show?
    • A) Singing
    • B) Dancing
    • C) Sports demonstrations
    • D) Magic tricks
    Show Answer C) Sports demonstrations
  3. What time will the Talent Show take place?
    • A) 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM
    • B) 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
    • C) 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
    • D) 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM
    Show Answer B) 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
  4. Where will the Talent Show be held?
    • A) School Gymnasium
    • B) Community Center
    • C) School Auditorium
    • D) School Cafeteria
    Show Answer C) School Auditorium
  5. What is the main purpose of Mr. Garcia's email?
    • A) To introduce new school rules
    • B) To invite parents to the School Talent Show
    • C) To inform about a new music program
    • D) To announce a school holiday
    Show Answer B) To invite parents to the School Talent Show
