Example 9:

Passage 1: Email

From: James Brown james.brown@hotel.com

To: All Staff

Subject: New Booking System Training

Dear Team,

We are pleased to announce that we will be using a new booking system, HotelBook. This system will help us manage room reservations more efficiently.

Training sessions will be held next week in the meeting room. Here is the schedule:

Monday, September 16: 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM
Wednesday, September 18: 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Please register at the front desk.

Thank you,

James Brown
Hotel Manager

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Staff

From: Robert Black, IT Department

Date: September 10

Subject: HotelBook System Preparation

Hello Team,

We will soon start using HotelBook, our new booking system. Before attending the training, please:

Save all current booking information on a USB drive.
Check if your computer has at least 500 MB of free space.
Download the HotelBook application from the company intranet: HotelBook Download

If you need help, contact the IT department at extension 301.

Best,

Robert Black
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the purpose of James Brown's email?
    • A. To announce a new hotel opening
    • B. To introduce a new booking system and training sessions
    • C. To provide updates on hotel policies
    • D. To invite staff to a meeting
    Show Answer B. To introduce a new booking system and training sessions
  2. When are the training sessions for HotelBook?
    • A. Monday and Tuesday
    • B. Wednesday and Thursday
    • C. Monday and Wednesday
    • D. Tuesday and Thursday
    Show Answer C. Monday and Wednesday
  3. What should staff do before the training according to Robert Black's memo?
    • A. Call the IT department
    • B. Save booking information, check computer space, and download the application
    • C. Attend a meeting
    • D. Update their contact information
    Show Answer B. Save booking information, check computer space, and download the application
  4. Why should staff save current booking information on a USB drive?
    • A. To share it with guests
    • B. To ensure it is not lost during the transition
    • C. To print it out later
    • D. To send it to the head office
    Show Answer B. To ensure it is not lost during the transition
  5. If staff have questions about HotelBook, what should they do?
    • A. Ask James Brown
    • B. Visit the front desk
    • C. Contact the IT department at extension 301
    • D. Email the manager
    Show Answer C. Contact the IT department at extension 301
