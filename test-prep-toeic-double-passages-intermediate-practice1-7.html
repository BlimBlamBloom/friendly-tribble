Example 7:

Passage 1: Email

From: Karen Adams karen.adams@marketingco.com

To: All Staff

Subject: New Marketing Analytics Tool Training

Dear Team,

We are pleased to announce the rollout of our new marketing analytics tool, MarketInsights. This tool will help us analyze marketing data more effectively and make informed decisions.

Training sessions will be held next week in the conference room. Here is the schedule:

Monday, February 15: 1:00 PM - 2:30 PM
Wednesday, February 17: 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM
Friday, February 19: 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM

Attendance is mandatory for all marketing staff. Please register for one of the sessions via the company intranet.

Thank you for your cooperation.

Best regards,

Karen Adams
Marketing Director

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Marketing Staff

From: Steve Miller, IT Department

Date: February 8

Subject: Preparation for MarketInsights Implementation

Dear Colleagues,

As we prepare to implement MarketInsights, our new marketing analytics tool, please complete the following steps before attending the training sessions:

Back up all current marketing data.
Ensure your workstation meets the system requirements:
Operating System: Windows 10 or macOS 10.14 or later
Memory: At least 8 GB RAM
Disk Space: Minimum of 5 GB available

Download and install the MarketInsights software from the company’s intranet: MarketInsights Download

If you encounter any issues during the installation, contact IT support at extension 1400.

Thank you for your attention.

Best,

Steve Miller
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the main purpose of Karen Adams' email?
    • A) To announce a company event
    • B) To introduce a new marketing analytics tool and provide training details
    • C) To inform about new marketing strategies
    • D) To provide a schedule for marketing meetings
    Show Answer B) To introduce a new marketing analytics tool and provide training details
  2. Who is required to attend the training sessions according to Karen Adams' email?
    • A) All staff members
    • B) Only IT department staff
    • C) Only sales staff
    • D) All marketing staff
    Show Answer D) All marketing staff
  3. According to Steve Miller's memo, what should staff do before attending the training sessions?
    • A) Update their personal information
    • B) Complete an online survey
    • C) Back up marketing data, verify system requirements, and download the software
    • D) Schedule a meeting with their supervisor
    Show Answer C) Back up marketing data, verify system requirements, and download the software
  4. Why is it important for staff to ensure their workstation meets the system requirements before the training sessions?
    • A) To enhance workstation performance
    • B) To ensure compatibility with the MarketInsights software
    • C) To comply with company policy
    • D) To prepare for a system upgrade
    Show Answer B) To ensure compatibility with the MarketInsights software
  5. If staff have issues with the installation process of MarketInsights, what should they do?
    • A) Ask Karen Adams
    • B) Register for another training session
    • C) Contact IT support at extension 1400
    • D) Visit the conference room
    Show Answer C) Contact IT support at extension 1400
