Example 1:

Passage 1: Email

From: Tom Davis tom.davis@supermarket.com

To: All Employees

Subject: New Check-Out System Training

Dear Team,

We are excited to introduce a new check-out system called QuickScan. This system will speed up the checkout process and reduce errors.

Training sessions will be held next week in the training room. The schedule is as follows:

Monday, July 8: 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM
Wednesday, July 10: 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Please sign up at the reception desk.

Best regards,

Tom Davis
Store Manager

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Employees

From: Jane Smith, IT Department

Date: July 5

Subject: QuickScan System Preparation

Hello Team,

We will soon start using QuickScan, our new check-out system. Before the training, please do the following:

Back up all current sales data on an external drive.
Make sure your computer has at least 1 GB of free space.
Download the QuickScan software from the internal network: QuickScan Download

For any questions, contact the IT department at extension 202.

Best,

Jane Smith
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the purpose of Tom Davis' email?
    • A. To announce a new store opening
    • B. To introduce a new check-out system and training sessions
    • C. To provide holiday schedules
    • D. To share new employee policies
    Show Answer B. To introduce a new check-out system and training sessions
  2. When are the training sessions for QuickScan?
    • A. Monday and Tuesday
    • B. Wednesday and Thursday
    • C. Monday and Wednesday
    • D. Tuesday and Thursday
    Show Answer C. Monday and Wednesday
  3. What should employees do before the training according to Jane Smith's memo?
    • A. Call the IT department
    • B. Back up sales data, check computer space, and download the software
    • C. Attend a meeting
    • D. Update their contact information
    Show Answer B. Back up sales data, check computer space, and download the software
  4. Why should employees back up sales data on an external drive?
    • A. To share it with customers
    • B. To prevent data loss during the transition
    • C. To print it out later
    • D. To send it to the head office
    Show Answer B. To prevent data loss during the transition
  5. If employees have questions about QuickScan, what should they do?
    • A. Ask Tom Davis
    • B. Visit the reception desk
    • C. Contact the IT department at extension 202
    • D. Email the store manager
    Show Answer C. Contact the IT department at extension 202
