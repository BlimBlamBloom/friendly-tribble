Example 4:

Passage 1: Email

From: Linda Parker linda.parker@globalservices.com

To: All Staff

Subject: Introduction of New Communication Platform

Dear Team,

I am excited to announce that we will be implementing a new communication platform called TeamChat. This platform is designed to improve our internal communication and collaboration.

To ensure everyone is comfortable with the new system, training sessions will be held next week in the training center. The schedule is as follows:

Tuesday, November 17: 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM
Thursday, November 19: 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM
Friday, November 20: 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM

Attendance is mandatory for all department heads and team leaders. Other team members are highly encouraged to attend. Please register for a session using the company intranet portal.

Thank you for your cooperation.

Best regards,

Linda Parker
Operations Manager

Passage 2: Memo

To: All Staff

From: David Wilson, IT Department

Date: November 10

Subject: Preparations for TeamChat Implementation

Dear Team,

As we prepare to implement TeamChat, our new communication platform, please complete the following steps before attending the training sessions:

Back up all current communication logs and documents.
Verify that your workstation meets the following system requirements:
Operating System: Windows 10 or macOS 10.14 or later
Memory: At least 4 GB RAM
Disk Space: Minimum of 2 GB available

Download the TeamChat software installer from the company’s intranet: TeamChat Download

Should you encounter any issues during the installation process, please reach out to the IT support team at extension 1800.

Thank you for your attention to these important preparations.

Best,

David Wilson
IT Department

Questions:

  1. What is the main purpose of Linda Parker's email?
    • A) To announce a company meeting
    • B) To introduce a new communication platform and provide training details
    • C) To inform about a new company policy
    • D) To provide a schedule for upcoming projects
    Show Answer B) To introduce a new communication platform and provide training details
  2. Who is required to attend the training sessions according to Linda Parker's email?
    • A) All staff members
    • B) Only IT staff
    • C) Only department heads and team leaders
    • D) Only project managers
    Show Answer C) Only department heads and team leaders
  3. According to David Wilson's memo, what should staff do before attending the training sessions?
    • A) Update their personal information
    • B) Complete an online survey
    • C) Back up communication logs, verify system requirements, and download the software
    • D) Schedule a meeting with their supervisor
    Show Answer C) Back up communication logs, verify system requirements, and download the software
  4. Why is it important for staff to verify their workstation meets the system requirements before the training sessions?
    • A) To enhance computer performance
    • B) To ensure compatibility with the TeamChat software
    • C) To comply with company policy
    • D) To prepare for a system upgrade
    Show Answer B) To ensure compatibility with the TeamChat software
  5. If staff have issues with the installation process of TeamChat, what should they do?
    • A) Ask Linda Parker
    • B) Register for another training session
    • C) Contact IT support at extension 1800
    • D) Visit the training center
    Show Answer C) Contact IT support at extension 1800
